Job Title = Technician

*Description/Job Summary*

Under the direction and supervision of the Patient Equipment Managers, the Patient Equipment Technician is based in the Facility Services Department and assists the hospital in provision of patient equipment by performing equipment delivery and retrieval duties in the following areas: Inpatient Nursing Units, Emergency Department, Admitting and other departments as directed. The Equipment Specialist will perform cleaning functions as assigned and/or as necessary.

*B. ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS* include but are not limited to the following

1. Maintains safe behavior per the Hospital s strict safety guidelines.

2. Adheres to the standards, policies and procedures of the Hospital, following safety guidelines for self, patients, visitors and other employees.

3. Adheres to dress code and badge identification policies as required by the hospital.

4. Adheres to the HIPAA standards for protecting patient information.

5. Adheres to the guidelines provided within the multiple standard work documents supplied to them.

6. Transports equipment in a manner complying with established LPCH & SHC guidelines.

7. Answers iPhones using all appropriate functions of the EPIC Transport Rover system and delivers equipment requests within the timeline specified in the Equipment Technician Guide book.

8. Communicates effectively in a clear, concise manner.

9. Demonstrates ability to organize workload by setting priorities.

*PEMS TECHNICIAN I:*

10. Contributes to the unit, department and hospital goals.

11. Meets work agreements and responsibilities as defined within the PEMS Technicians Guide book.

12. Adheres to the HIPAA standards for protecting patient information.

13. Clean, pickup, distribute and store routine inventory items in accordance with established guidelines/policies or as directed.

14. Perform daily rounds twice per shift in consonance with the PEMS Tech I Rounding standard work document.

15. Pickup and/or deliver Dialysis & ITA equipment as required.

16. Log-out/tag-out broken equipment, ensure work orders are generated for the appropriate repair entity and facilitate transfer of these items to appropriate repair location.

17. Ensure the prompt delivery (15 minutes of less) of high-priority Respiratory Therapy equipment.

18. Keeps PEMS work areas neat and organized with the equipment at the lowest possible profile.

19. Elevates customer service complaints/issues promptly to the PEMS management staff.

20. Logs out of Epic Rover 15 minutes prior to the end of their shift to complete all end-of-shift tasks and job requirements, check work e-mail and complete their assigned reports.

21. Does not engage in personal tasks during their work shift unless on their 15-minute break or lunch, including but not limited to, use of a personal cell phone or other personal electronics, eating, or using the internet.

*Job Overview:*

The Technician may work in any type of location on client premises. This individual performs maintenance on various types of machines. The general responsibilities of the position include those listed below, but Sodexo may identify other responsibilities of the position. These responsibilities may differ among accounts, depending on business necessities and client requirements.

* Disassembles machines and repairs or replaces broken parts.

* Orders replacement parts.

* Inspects completed repairs.

* Complies with all company safety and risk management policies and procedures.

* Reports all accidents and injuries in a timely manner.

* Applies all applicable OSHA and related local safety requirements to all assigned work.
