Making your next move count
Maintain, repair and service customer production equipment and systems. Main tasks and responsibilities
Machine parts to specifications, using machine tools, such as manual and CNC lathes, milling machines, shapers, or grinders to manufacture costume pins,bushing,and new parts
Study sample parts, blueprints, drawings, or engineering information to determine methods or sequences of operations needed to fabricate products
Measure dimensions of completed products or workpieces to verify conformance to specification What you'll need to succeed:
Ability to fabricate and modify parts to make or repair machine tools or maintain industrial machines, applying knowledge of mechanics, mathematics, metal properties, layout, and machining procedures
Highly proficient operating manual lathes, CNC lathe machine and milling equipment
Ability to read and understand blue prints and schematics
Ability to calculate dimensions of workpieces, products, or equipment in metrics and calculate dimensions or tolerances, using instruments such as micrometers or vernier calipers
Ability to work overtime as required You will be part of:
