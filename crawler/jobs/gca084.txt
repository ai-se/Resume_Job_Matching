Test completed equipment to detect and remove defects
Perform routine maintenance on machinery as required
Calculate dimensions and tolerance using measuring instruments
Occasional CNC Machine operation
Qualifications:
Previous experience in machinery such as lathes and Milling,Drill press and associated equipment
Familiarity machine tools and equipment
Strong analytical and mathematical skills
Strong attention to detail
CNC Machining a plus
