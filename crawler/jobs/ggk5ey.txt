Position Description
Day Machine Systems is looking for a hard working individual with the desire to run complex manufacturing operations.

Duties and Responsibilities
Operators are required to:
Use blueprints to ensure proper dimensions on finished parts.
Troubleshoot CNC malfunctions and alert proper personnel when necessary.
Use inspection equipment such as micrometers, calipers etc.
Make offsets frequently
Change tooling frequently
Run multiple machines when cycle time permits

Requirements
Must have a High School Diploma or GED or equivalent work experience.
Experience using inspection equipment such as micrometers, calipers etc.
5 years minimum experience running CNC Equipment
Ability to lift up to 30 lbs when necessary.
A desire to learn.
