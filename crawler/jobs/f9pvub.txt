External Description:Job DescriptionThe machinist is responsible for the planning, set-up, operation, and adjustment of both manual and CNC controlled shop equipment. Machinists utilize work orders and drawings to perform production tasks for our gasket and seal products.ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

Utilizing shop equipment (lathes, grinders, mills, etc.) to machine gaskets and seals

Set-up and adjustment of machines based on work orders and drawings

Measurement of products using micrometers and calipers

Scheduling and execution of work based on work orders and routers

Transacting machined products to next stage in production utilizing Oracle system

Maintaining a safe, clean, and hazard free work area

Comply with all OSHA and company safety practices and regulations

Drives results - Consistently achieving results, even under tough circumstances.

Decision quality - Making good and timely decisions that keep the organization moving forward
JOB QUALIFICATIONS:

High School Diploma or Equivalent

Ability to read and interpret drawings and work orders

Previous experience working with machining equipment is a plus

Ability to work independently and with minimal direction or supervision

Commitment to work safely in all aspects of the position
