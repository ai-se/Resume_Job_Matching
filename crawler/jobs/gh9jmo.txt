Job Title = Technician

Our Technicians have exciting hands-on positions in which they build lasers and laser components. These positions require knowledge of electronics and optics theory, assembly, testing, and maintaining circuitry with electronic and optic components.

Duties:

* Perform a full range of technical assembly including soldering, small piece installations, fiber handling, inspections, cleaning, measuring and use of magnification equipment

* Ability to accurately follow IPG manufacturing processes which may include repetitive tasks

* Strong documentation skills and attention to details

* Excellent manual dexterity

* Computer skills including MS Windows, Excel, and Word

* Comfortable working in a cleanroom environment on a consistent and regular basis

* AS in electronics preferred, electro-mechanical or electro-optics or equivalent experience required

* Fiber handling, electronics assembly, splicing, component level assembly experience helpful

* IPC-610 certified preferred

* Ability to read and follow written instructions and schematics.

* Strong communication skills required for interaction within group.

* Regular and reliable attendance at work is an essential function of this job.

* Ability to work overtime during the week and occasional weekends as required by production

This position must meet Export Control compliance requirements, therefore a "US Person" as defined by 22C.F.R. §120.15 are required. "US Person" includes US Citizen, lawful permanent resident, refugee, asylee.
