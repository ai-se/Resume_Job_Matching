Job Title = Machinist

JOB SUMMARY:
The Machine Operator is responsible for a variety of basic manufacturing tasks, including assembly work, tending/operating machines, sorting, basic grinding, etc. This individual will have an opportunity to develop and acquire additional skilled manufacturing trades.
REPORTING TO:
Plant Manager
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:
Support all local manufacturing operations
Operate general equipment
Complete relevant paperwork following GDP/GMP guidelines
Manually clean all portable equipment and small parts
Perform progressive machining and assembly operations on apparatus, equipment and limited range of experimental and development of work
Set up and operate various types of machine tools, perform floor assembly and erection operations, and hand operations such as filling, fitting, tapping, scraping, etc.
Perform any other miscellaneous work as required
SKILLS and ABILITIES:
Strong mechanical aptitude
Utilize basic measuring tools
Ability to speak and read in English
EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE:
High school diploma or GED equivalent preferred
1+ years' experience in a manufacturing environment required
