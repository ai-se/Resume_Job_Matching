Job Title = Engineer

Austin Proper Hotel & Residences is looking for a skilled Engineer. This 32-story modern high-rise will include 244 rooms and a limited collection of 99 branded luxury residences and penthouse for sale. Amenities include two rooftop pools, wellness center and spa and three food & beverage destinations driven by McGuire Moorman Hospitality.

Reporting to the Director of Engineering, the Engineer will be responsible for the maintenance efforts including the physical aspects of the building, HVAC, electrical, plumbing, carpentry, major appliances. The Engineer maintains preventative maintenance within the rooms to ensure they are being kept up to standards on a regular basis and the proper documentation is being recorded.

Job Overview
• Respond to maintenance requests received from the Front Desk or Housekeeping
• Diagnose problems or repairs machines
• Complete work orders in a timely and efficient manner
• Inspect and test machinery and equipment
• Conduct preventative maintenance relating to all property's units, public areas & amenities
• Maintain focus on a high-level of guest satisfaction
• Provide a warm, authentic greeting to all residents and guests according to company standards
• Work effectively in a fast-paced environment and maintain composure at all times

Qualifications
• Minimum 1-3 years’ experience in a luxury or lifestyle hotel
• Knowledge about the mechanics of the hotel including; boiler systems, booster pump systems, building automation, electrical and plumbing systems
• Knowledge of painting, finish work and minor carpentry
• Must possess excellent interpersonal, analytical, and organizational skills
• Must be service-oriented and have a positive attitude

Education
High school diploma or equivalent.
