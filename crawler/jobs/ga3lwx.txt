Job Title = Machinist

Description:
As part of a small, passionate and accomplished team of experts, you will machine and fabricate flight vehicle components for various spaceflight systems. You will share in the team’s impact on all aspects of aerospace tolerance and materials while setting up and operating mills, lathes and multi-axis machines to produce accurate components enabling first article success on tight tolerance high value parts. This position will directly impact the history of space exploration and will require your dedicated commitment and detailed attention towards safe and repeatable spaceflight.

Responsibilities:

A minimum of 10 years of experience in machining aerospace components.

Experience with conventional and CNC mills, lathes.

Excellent blue print reading skills including GD&T per ASME Y14.5M-1994 standard.

Proper cutter selection with appropriate materials.

Ability to provide Design for Manufacturing feedback.

Excellent communication skills.

Must have stainless steel and aluminum experience.

Fluent in use of precision hand tools.

Familiar with use of personal computers.

Self-motivation and ability to prioritize projects to meet completion dates.
