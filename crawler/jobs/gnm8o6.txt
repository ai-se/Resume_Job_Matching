Job Title = Engineer

This is an entry level engineering position. As a member of our engineering team, you will be trained to perform the following:
Design individual joist members and welds according to codes and recognized practices;
Provide engineering expertise to drafting team members and other company departments;
Interpret contract documents to ensure that all specific conditions are included in the fabrication of our products;
Resolve fabrication and field problems;
Check special designs, review drafting and shop order work, and sign-off on projects;
Prepare repair procedures and sketches of misfabrication or site conditions;
Maintain a positive work atmosphere by behaving and communicating in an effective manner with customers, coworkers and supervisors.

Bachelors degree in Civil Engineering preferred;
An understanding of engineering theories and principles desired.
