Job Title = Machinist

Summary:- We are looking for a skilled Machinist to operate a variety of machines to perform assigned tasks.- You will produce or repair precision parts using manual equipment and accurate measurements.- A great machinist needs to possess manual dexterity and an eye for detail.- They must also have knowledge of mathematics and mechanics as well as respect for safety precautions.Main Roles and Responsibilities:- Calculate dimensions and tolerances using knowledge of mathematics and instruments such as micrometers and vernier calipers.- Machine parts to specifications using machine tools such as lathes, milling machines, slotting machine and radial arm drill.- Measure, examine, and test completed units in order to detect defects and ensure conformance to specifications, using precision instruments such as micrometers.- Study sample parts, blueprints, drawings, and engineering information in order to determine methods and sequences of operations needed to fabricate products, and determine product dimensions and tolerances.- Service / Maintain all machines.- Ensure all work is carried out in accordance with company policies, procedures, standards and other work instructions.- Ensure that any problems with drawings or work instructions are reported to the Supervisor immediately.- Responsible for the General Housekeeping within the Department.- Execution of risk assessments and hazard assessments (START Cards) on all related Work Scopes.- Provide assistance to other workshop personnel as required.- Provide core competence on various machining operations.- Report any machine damage to Supervisor immediately.- Participate in any Risk Assessments/Toolbox Talks.- Ensure all Machine Shop QA Cards are filled out correctly.- Report any damaged tooling to the Supervisor.Health, Safety and Quality:- Responsible for the safety of all workshop personnel.- Ensure that all Company Health, Safety and Environmental procedures and policies are being adhered to at all times in the workshop.- Help with the general housekeeping of the workshop and surrounding areas.Training and People:- Communicate effectively and positively with Team Leader/ Deputy Team Leader at all times.- Positive approach towards all scopes of work and be flexible to change.- Ability to work effectively as a Team and as an Individual.- Willingness to support and develop others.- Active participation in development opportunities including VITAL and the Competency System.Qualifications and Experience:- High school diploma or equivalent; completion of vocational training or an apprenticeship will be an asset- Proven experience as machinist (3 months).- Ability to use precision tools (e.g.
calipers) to take accurate measurements- Knowledge of the properties of metal and other material- Ability to read blueprints, schematics and manualsSkills and Behaviours:- Honest and reliable- Ability to work well as a Team and take own initiative- Commitment to continual improvement and a flexible approach to change- Self motivated, enthusiastic and committed to delivering HSEQ Excellence- All relevant job features shall be as per relevant Competency Framework booklet.
