Job Title = Machinist

Ronan manufacturer is looking for a Machinist to join their growing team.
The company is searching for an individual with the desire to do precision work, learn a skill which is in high demand, and to grow professionally within their company.
This position will work directly with Senior Machinists and will be responsible for production of machined parts by programming, setting up, and operating a computer numerical control (CNC) machine, conventional lathes and mills and precision grinders.
On the job training will be provided while performing responsibilities on the machine shop floor.Duties:- Read prints and job orders to determine dimensions and machine set-up- Set-up and operate various machining equipment such as lathes or mills, in order to machine or grind components to precise customer specifications- Use measurement tools to ensure accuracy of must have a high school diploma or equivalent; have excellent math and mechanical skills/aptitude; and have the ability and desire to do precision work.
Previous experience with lathes or mills is preferred.
Candidate must be able to work off shifts once trained.Wage: Hourly, DOE
