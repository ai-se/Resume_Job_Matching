Job Description

Volt Workforce Solutions is seeking entry level operators for a large injection molding company. If you're looking to work at a stable company in Northern Colorado to gain experience in the plastic injection molding field, then this could be the right fit for you!
We are actively interviewing for multiple positions and multiple shifts. To be considered please submit your resume with contact information. Feel free to call or text .
Operators will be responsible for the following:

Completion of work orders and corresponding paperwork.

Assembly of customer product in a fast paced environment focusing on safety, quality, and productivity.

Operation of various equipment used in the processing of plastic.

Inspection, trimming, and packing of parts that adhere to customer requirements.

Maintain cleanliness of work area to support safety efforts and awareness.
