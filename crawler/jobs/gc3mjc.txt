Job Title = Machinist

This position is a CNC machinist. This person will be responsible for the production of parts in a job shop. They will be working with vertical mills. They will have to do 3-5 setups/change overs a day. Day to day they will need to know how to read a blueprint, go get the material that they need to make the part, and produce the part. This person is going to need to know how to do first article inspection on their parts and will be responsible for using quality tools (mics, cals, gauges) in order to do so. They need to be able to hold a tolerance of +/- .001. They will be working on vertical mill machining centers.

Location: South Bend, IN
Pay:$15- $19/hr
Shift: 3rd shift: 9:30pm to 6am but will train on days for a little bit
