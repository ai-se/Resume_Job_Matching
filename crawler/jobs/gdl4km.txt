The purpose of this position is:

+ To fabricate metal parts by operating CNC milling and multi-spindle machines using blueprints and design sketches to ensure the proper dimensions and tolerance levels of finished products.

+ Support daily operations of the Production Department.

This position includes significant duties such as:

+ Monitoring the operation and output of multiple automated CNC and dial adjusted multi-spindle turning/milling machines

+ Inspecting parts to blueprint specifications using various precision measuring instruments and gages

+ Performing tool checks on the machines, emptying scrap, cleaning, weighing, and packaging parts; other duties as outlined in the department documentation

+ Making machine adjustments, changing tooling, loading machines, and troubleshooting machine problems

+ Interpreting SPC Data and making machine adjustments to ensure process stability

+ Providing basic cleaning and maintenance of machines by completing maintenance checks and performing scheduled checks; identifying and resolving machining inconsistencies

+ Making decisions to shut off machines that are out of tolerance

+ Ensuring all product logs and quality records are complete

+ Assisting with meeting production goals by processing parts through ancillary equipment (e.g., chip checker, cleaner, etc.)

+ Responsible for correct raw material usage and associated inventory transactions

+ May provide guidance to employees to ensure part quality and efficiency

+ Monitoring computer screens during the testing process

+ Contributing to 5S departmental goals by maintaining the cleanliness of the production area

Working Conditions:

+ Good working conditions; frequently lifting up to 25 pounds, rarely up to 50 pounds; works with chemical substances, dust, noise, fumes odors, oil mist; requires use of PPE in identified areas

Education:

+ High School diploma or GED equivalent required; Advanced trades mathematics

Experience:

+ 3+ years in a mid-volume manufacturing environment; 2 years' experience with convection machine (live tooling) or multi-spindle machines; mechanical aptitude; ability to communicate effectively in English Other duties as required - e.g.

+ Able to set up and run 1st articles while troubleshooting all tooling issues to produce high quality component parts.

+ Experience with the operation of multiple CNC Lathes with Sub-spindle turning/Live tooling capabilities, bar feeders (HAAS and Fanuc control) and CNC mill experience with Fanuc Control.

+ Knowledge of basic and or advanced Programming Skills, Esprit CAM software knowledge a plus
