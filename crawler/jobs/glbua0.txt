Job Title = Machine Operator

Job Details

Description

The Livonia Distribution Center, a 400,000 sq. ft. refrigerated facility operating 24/7; is currently seeking a Machine Operator to join our team.
Full Time Shift: A2 Sunday-Tuesday and alternating Wednesdays; 5:15pm-5:15am
Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
Safely operate the production machinery at the highest possible efficiency while tracking production and scrap.
Communicate any problems with the machines, product or the department to the shift supervisor.
Tracks all pertinent information: initial scrap, product quality, cases produced.
Runs the production machine in accordance to customer specifications, while following pertinent SQF regulations.
Position Requirements:
High School degree or equivalent is required.
Experience in the produce industry is preferred
Must pass Drug Test and Background Check
Working Conditions:
Work in our refrigerated warehouse, 50-degree controlled temperature zones
Ability to stand and walk for long periods of time
Lift up to 30 lbs. with or without accommodations

Medical, Dental, Vision, Life Insurance
401K
Paid time off and holiday pay
