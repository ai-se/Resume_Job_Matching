Machine Operator

We currently have multiple openings at a well known label printing and lamination manufacturing company located in Bensalem, PA. This position involves working in a fast paced production environment.

Shift times run 8AM - 4:30PM, 4PM - 12:30AM, and 12:00AM - 8:30AM. All shifts require working weekends as a part of a 5 day work week. Consecutive off days will run during the week. Must be flexible to work every Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. Starting pay rate is $15.00 - $16.00 per hour based on experience. All positions are on a contingent basis. Employees with great attendance and job performance will be eligible for permanent hire after probationary period.

Responsibilities:

The Machine Operator will be responsible for running lamination and print press machines daily, perform machine set ups, run inspections every hour, create and complete job order batch record documentation as needed on the production line.

Working hours: 6:00 AM - 2:30 PM

Skills:

The ideal candidate will have prior experience working in a fast paced work environment, have previous industrial machine operation experience, and practice basic reading, writing, and math skills.
