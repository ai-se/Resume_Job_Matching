Job Title = Machine Operator

Responsibilities for this Machine Operator job include but are not limited to:
Studying and interpreting drawings, manuals, specifications or sample parts
Operation of a CNC machine, previous experience preferred
Participating and supporting the 5S process
Adjusting controls of machines to ensure quality of work
Communicating with supervisors, engineers, production control and quality to resolve quality issues
Candidates must meet the following requirements to be considered for this Machine Operator job:
Must pass a felony and misdemeanor background screening
Ability to lift objects up to 50 lbs and stand for 8-hour shifts
General mechanical knowledge and ability to operate small hand tools
Ability to accept and follow directions, attention to detail, and follow all established safety protocols
