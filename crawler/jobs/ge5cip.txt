HOW YOU'LL MAKE A DIFFERENCE:

* Learn and teach Lean Manufacturing to all plant personnel. Lead Lean Manufacturing actions throughout the plant to include Kaizen Events, Point Kaizens, projects, 3P Events, etc. Work with the Douglas Dynamics Milwaukee facility to share improvements and best practices.

* Utilize CAD, CAM and CAE software to generate tooling, fixtures, gauges and equipment drawings. Ability to fabricate and/or have built any item that is designed and deemed useful to accomplish plant goals and objectives.

* Ability to program profiling machines, fabrication equipment, automated welding equipment, robots, etc. Provide expertise to properly service and maintain all plant equipment and tooling.

* Implement Standardized Work in all areas of the plant. Write SOWs, train personnel, audit personnel and provide feedback to plant supervision.

* Use creativity to introduce new technology, methodology, processes, tooling, gauges, fixtures and equipment into the plant as part of continuous improvement.

* Responsible for Engineering Change Notice updates for specific areas of the plant.

* Work with Sales and Marketing and Technical Services to support initiatives to improve customer relations.

* Participate when feasible with teams formed to create continuous improvement efforts in systems and procedures.

WHAT THIS ROLE NEEDS:

Education and/or Experience: BS degree in a technical field with 5 10 years of applicable experience, MET preferred or PE certification with 7 12 years of applicable experience. Experience in metal fabrications and welding environments

Communication Skills: Must be able to make effective presentations to groups of managers, supervisors, engineers and other employees. Ability to communicate effectively both verbally and in writing on complex abstract issues, contracts and specifications, technical issues.

Computer Skills: Knowledge of and ability to apply computers and related software, such as spreadsheet, word processing, CAD, etc., to the solution of engineering problems.
