Job Title = Engineer

Acara solutions is looking for Engineer for our Client located in Jackson, MI -Functionally responsible to support the High Performance Work team including day to day floor support, tooling design, gauging, quality, and efficiency.
-Process improvement through collection and analysis manufacturing data to improve safety, product quality, customer delivery, and cost reductions.
-Provide on-floor process information for training and meeting engineering standards.
-Support and provide production associates the tools required to perform the job in a manner to meet all requirements.
Support special process and customer audits.
-Maintain and Improve safe and environmentally responsible manufacturing processes -Uses MESH (client Environmental, Safety and Health process), and other client and industry standard practices.
-Designs processes to ensure that components and assemblies meet all internal and external customer quality specifications (client Quality System, AS9100, and NADCAP). -Ensures efficiency tied to capital intensity and human resources by utilizing client LEAN and Six Sigma tools -Active membership of a High Performance Work Team (HPWT)/Focus Factory to support on-time delivery of customer orders through the manufacturing process excellence, including troubleshooting machine and tooling problems, supporting the DMR process, and implementing continuous flow concepts.
-Maintain and improve product flow through the plant through reduced Defective material -Report (DMR) and Process stop occurrences.
-Resolve issue quickly when product stops occur.
-Collect and analyze manufacturing data and metrics to aid in design and improvement of processes or products including scrap and defective material report (DMR) system.
-Provide functional and technical support to the HPWT within the assigned area of focus as well as the organization as required to support goals.
-This position needs use of information or access to hardware which is subject to the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR). Required Skills / Qualifications: -Bachelor's degree in Engineering -Minimum 1 year of engineering, operations, or manufacturing experience Preferred Skills / Qualifications: -Bachelor degree in mechanical, industrial, or aerospace engineering -Minimum 3 years of engineering, operations, or manufacturing experience -Experience with Nadcap (or other industry standards) audits.
-Previous lean and/or six sigma certification, with demonstrated results -Experience in high mix/low volume manufacturing environment.
