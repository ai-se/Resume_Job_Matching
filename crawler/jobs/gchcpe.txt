Job Title = Engineer

CertTech, a premier provider of test automation services, test systems and products solutions, is seeking all levels of engineering professionals in the Systems, Software, Electrical and Mechanical disciplines for our Albuquerque operations. CertTech is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company based in the Kansas City metro area with branch offices in Albuquerque, NM and Boulder, CO.

Job Summary: Contribute to the design, development and integration of hardware, software, and systems in a team environment. Responsible for preparation of deliverables such as planning documents, requirements documents, design documents, system designs/drawings, software designs and source code, hardware designs/drawings, review records, calibration and alignment procedures, measurement accuracy statements, reports and other responsibilities as directed by management.

Skills/Experience:

Excellent technical communications skills

Solid client interaction skills

And one or more of:

Familiarity/experience with modular instrumentation (National Instruments, Keysight, etc…)

LabVIEW Realtime/FPGA and/or VHDL a plus

National Instruments certification (CLD, CLA) a plus

Experience with National Instruments TestStand, Keysight VEE

National Instruments certification (CTD, CTA) a plus

Familiarity/experience with C/C++/C#, VB/VB.NET, Object-Oriented Programming

Familiarity/experience with Instrumentation and Test Automation

Familiarity/experience with PXIe/AXIe, Data Acquisition, Virtual Instrumentation

Basic understanding of the fundamentals of verification and validation

Responsibilities:

Professional interaction with clients/partners

Designing, developing and maintaining test and data acquisition/control systems

Status reporting

Troubleshooting system level, software, hardware, wiring, and interfaces

Supporting new business development activities

Minimum Requirements:

College Degree: physics, computer science, electrical/mechanical/systems engineering or related field

U.S. citizenship

Ability to obtain a DOE security clearance. Existing security clearance a plus

CertTech Mission Statement:
To be a key strategic supplier of innovative products, systems and services supporting development of advanced technology high reliability solutions.
