SUMMARY

Freeman is seeking a talented, enthusiastic IT Support Technician who will give our clients the best internal and external service in the industry. Under direct supervision, the IT Support Technician performs general maintenance tasks, troubleshoots and repairs computer systems and peripheral equipment located throughout the branch. Maintains an adequate spare parts inventory of systems, subsystems, and component parts used in repair work. Additional responsibilities include:

* Maintains systems, performs maintenance and resolves technical issues.

* Provides branch office program support as required.

* Provides show site support in setting up computers and installing software packages and updates.

* Provides user training for hardware/software products; identifies problems and resolves hardware/software/network malfunctions; performs minor hardware/software/network maintenance, hardware installation/replacement.

REQUIRMENTS

* Minimum two (2) + years of increasingly complex and progressive experience in computer systems/network support; includes two years of specialized experience related to the task described above

* Fundamental knowledge of general networking and Microsoft/Novell networking

* Must have proven troubleshooting skills

* Must have demonstrated project management and organizational skills

* Needs to have knowledge of configurations for workstations, peripherals, and cabling

Minimum Requirements

EDUCATION

* High School Diploma or GED

PREFERRED

* Associates Degree
