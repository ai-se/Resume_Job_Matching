Job Title:: Technician

Roles and Responsibilities:

" Build and assist with construction of electro-mechanical test fixtures and engineering prototypes

" Build and assist with construction of prototype flexible endoscopes and endoscopic tools

" Perform other engineering technician duties as-needed

Skill/Job Requirements:

" Experience with mechanical prototyping and test of flexible devices

" Hands-on capability performing compact assembly tasks including soldering, using the hot box, dremel, braider, etc.

" Proven ability to work well in team environment including teaching techniques to other technicians and learning from other technicians

" Proficiency with computers and Excel software
