**job description**

Machine Operator

Want to work in a friendly environment with an established company driven by their core values? We are currently in need of Machine Operators for all shifts at our Madison, Georgia location. Qualified candidates will possess a positive attitude, a drive to do things right the first time, and sense of urgency to get things done! This job is fast paced and quality driven.

Responsibilities:

Interpretation and recognition of quality expectations in order to meet customer quality.

Physical removal of parts & proper handling from any injection molding/blow molding presses.

Insertion of parts into any secondary fixture, operation of any secondary fixture, physical removal and visual inspection of part to ensure process has been done correctly.

Perform written record keeping to ensure quality standards have been met.

Packing, counting, weighing, labeling and moving parts either manually or with automated assistance as required.

Maintain an acceptable attendance record.

Working hours: 7:00 AM - 7:00 AM

Skills:

Standing or walking for periods up to eight hours at a time.

Reaching, feeling, handling or grasping and detecting part inconsistencies and variances by sight and touch 8 hours or more at a time.

Accurately distinguish colors and shades of colors.

Lifting, carrying, pushing, pulling 40 pounds or less.

Twisting and turning to gain access to gaylords and packing crates intermittently during an 8 hour shift.

Education:

High School

Experience:

Experienced

Qualifications:

Ability to perform basic math functions (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and fractions).

Written and verbal communication skills.

This job requires attention to detail and working at an extremely fast pace.

Ability to work independently without a need for excessive supervision.
