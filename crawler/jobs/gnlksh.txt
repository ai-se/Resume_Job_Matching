Technician
Monday - Friday 7:00 a.m. - 3:30 p.m.
12 month position; paying $15.00 per hour.You will provide testing support of roofing component products. Strict adherence to safety and good laboratory practices are a must. Knowledge of Instron test devices a plus.
JOB DESCRIPTION
Performs laboratory and application testing and applies understanding of other laboratory test methods to effectively test products.
Person will also be responsible for accurately testing and reporting data.
Ideal candidate will have familiarity with using lab balances, reading micrometers, writing operating procedures, and following a testing plan.
QUALIFICATIONS
The right candidate must understand and use basic statistics and be comfortable using a computer.
A working knowledge of MS Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook and PowerPoint) required.
Minimum of an Associated Degree is required, preferably in science or engineering.
1-2 Years experience in an R&D lab or quality lab required.
