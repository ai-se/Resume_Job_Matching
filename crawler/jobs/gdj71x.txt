Job Title = Machinist

We are seeking a Machinist to join our team!
Responsibilities:
Capable of running production parts on CNC live tool lathes and 5 axis mills.
Calculate dimensions and tolerances using measuring instruments.
Must be able to read, interpret and work from engineering prints, drawings, and sketches.
Must be capable of Set-ups.
Must be able to inspect and be responsible for the quality of your own work.
Qualifications:
3-5 yrs prior experience or trade school training.
Read and comprehend engineering drawings.
Check dimensions including GDT call-outs, calculate True Position in inch and metric and standards.
Ability to lift 70 lbs to waist height.
