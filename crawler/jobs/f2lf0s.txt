Job Title = Machinist

Employee Type:
Full-Time
Location:
Cleveland, OH
Job Type:
Manufacturing, Engineering
Experience:
At least 3 year(s)
Date Posted:

Job DescriptionPerforms manual machining functions on many types of machinery (i.e.: lathes, grinders, mills, saws, etc.) to support the manufacturing of tools, dies, fixtures and gauges
Responsible for supporting the construction of several products concurrently.
Interpret design blueprints to determine manufacturing plan
Perform operations within very close tolerances.
Have their own tools and toolbox
Ability to inspect own work.
Ability to work with Engineering and Programmingthroughout the manufacturing process
Able to operate multiple machinery; (Bridge Port, Grinders, Saws, EDM).
Comply with Corporate EHS, Quality and HR rules and standards
Qualifications
Ability to prioritize and handle multiple assignments.
Ability to effectively work / communicate as part of a die making team.
Ability to independently evaluate design specifications.
Basic Math and Trigonometry knowledge
Experience with general machining practices in a job shop environment
Five or more years of precision machining experience:
a) Mold making experience is a plus
b) Knowledge of custom tooling is a plus
Education:
High school graduate along with secondary machining training.
