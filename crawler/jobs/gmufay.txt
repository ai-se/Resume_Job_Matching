Machine Operator
Are you an experienced Machine Operator looking for work in the Concord area? Are you a reliable candidate who thrives in warehouse roles?
Randstad is looking for you! We have an available position in Concord operating on 12 hour shifts and rotation schedule.
Responsibilities:
As a machine operator, you will be operating a slitting machine that will cut up large roles to cut down to specific customer requirements. This machine operator will be responsible for meeting a certain production amount every day for the amount of rolls that will be cut to be shipped out. In this position, you will be measured by the percentage of line items you correctly cut and your efficiency.
Working hours: 5:45 AM - 6:45 PM
Skills:
Must be able to follow written and verbal instructions
Must be able to enter production data into computer
Production line experience in manufacturing
Must be able to lift multiple pounds repeatedly throughout shift
Set up and operate slitting machines according to the appropriate "machine operator's manual" under direction
Carry and supply materials to the machines
Remove finished product from the machine. Supplies and finished goods of up to 38 lbs.
Investigate and analyze causes of target frustration
Help train other machine operators
Education:
High School
Experience:
Experienced
Qualifications:
Must have a least one year of experience as a machine operator
Must be able to operate a machine solely
