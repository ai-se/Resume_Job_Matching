Job Title = Engineer

Description:

Position Summary:

The Engineer is responsible for participating in logistics analysis and engineering work involving all aspects of transportation design. They solve problems presented to them to support customers, development activities or request for proposals by designing, developing, analyzing or implementing comprehensive supply chain solutions. They collect data, perform transportation, statistical and mapping analysis, design customized solutions and work on a team that is developing or implementing the processes and service solutions for the customer. Might implement and design customized solutions, use engineering tools for modeling or utilize the tools or solutions for implementation. They use

methods or tools under the guidance of their manager to create models, tools or processes that will be used by Analysts and Operations daily. They are participant in location startups,process improvement teams and cost saving initiatives.

Major Responsibilities:

Conduct logistics analysis in support of both operational and new sales projects.

Conduct the development and implementation of engineering solutions, including programming/software development.

Provide maintenance and support activities for all existing solutions, including software maintenance as needed.

Preparation of technical and user documentation and presentations for all solutions.

Designs, implements or analyzes one customer solution at a time with supervision as assigned

Works effectively as a team member accomplishing assigned goals

Participates in cross-functional groups to create the expected customer outcomes

Interacts with internal and external customers on an as needed basis

Supports the preparation of results into proposal documents or other engineering reports and presentations for customers and management

Helps ensure a solution will meet financial goals and provide cost savings

Meets customer deadlines by providing required designs and analysis

Develops an understanding of the customers' needs and take actions to ensure that such needs are met

Understands what the customer does, what their business model is and what their expectations are

Able to resolve situations quickly, communicate status of problem, and work with the appropriate subject matter expert until it is fixed with the guidance of a more senior level associate

Other projects and tasks as assigned by supervisor

Qualifications:

2 to 5 years comparable experience designing, developing, implementing and executing supply chain solutions.

Bachelors degree in Industrial Engineering, Supply Chain, Logistics, Management Science, Computer Science or other quantitative discipline required. Master s Degree preferred.

Fundamental understanding of transportation, warehousing, supply chain and logistics concepts

Effective communication skills both written and verbal, and with internal and external customers required.

Must be proficient with the Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Access, Word and PowerPoint) as well as Minitab. Skills in MS Project and Visio a plus.

Intermediate computer skills in supply chain network modeling design tools preferred

Ability to analyze data and conduct extensive research required

Excellent analytical/problem solving capabilities

Developing presentation skills

Must demonstrate aptitude for developing written, oral, and interpersonal communications skills, particularly when problems arise that may affect solution outcome and/or project schedule.

Customer orientation

Team player

Must be able to work somewhat independently and concurrently on a range of unstructured tasks/projects and properly prioritize work, all with some supervision or direction

Time management and multi-tasking skills

Regular, predictable, full attendance is an essential function of the job

Willingness to travel as necessary, work the required schedule, work at the specific location required, complete Penske employment application, submit to a background investigation (to include past employment, education, and criminal history) and drug screening are required.
