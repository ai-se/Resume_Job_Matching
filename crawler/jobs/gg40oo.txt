Job Title = Technician

We have immediate openings available for several technical roles supporting engineering activities within Sensata's Aerospace, Automotive, and Heavy Vehicle & Off-Road (HVOR) business units. All of these technical roles are located at our Attleboro, MA facility and offer unique opportunities for all selected candidates. In these roles, candidates will work in a dynamic environment in support of various activities ranging from mechanical & electrical design, product testing and characterization as well as process optimization, troubleshooting, and root cause analysis. If you have a technical background and possess skills that could be leveraged in support of these activities, you are encouraged to apply.

Available Positions Include:

* Environmental Testing lab Technician

* Calibration Lab Technician

* Mechanical Engineering Technician

* Electrical Engineering Technician

* Dimensional Analysis Lab Technician

* Metallography Lab Technician

* Materials Technician

* Electro-Mechanical Technician

* General Responsibilities:

* Assist in new product design development and design upgrades to existing products

* Perform engineering tests

* Acquire, analyze, and document data.

* Develop fixtures.

* Run electrical, mechanical, and product evaluation studies.

* Make recommendations to improve manufacture of new and existing products

* The ability to perform device failure analysis if needed

* Ability to read prints and machine fixtures, tooling, etc. from those prints or from a Solidworks model. Experience on CNC machines highly desired.

* Support and oversee the build of prototype samples, including developing work instructions, guiding operators, collecting and evaluating in-process data, and making final judgments on product quality prior to shipment to customer.

* Verify compliance of incoming prototype material prior to assembly builds

* Perform studies to validate internal measurement systems (Gage R&R, MSA)

* Conduct designed experiments to understand process variables

* Run tests to validate that specifications are met

* Be able to interpret data and make observations/recommendations from experimental results

* Must have working knowledge of basic statistics (mean, sigma, Cp, Cpk, etc.)

* Required Skills:

* Associated degree in a technical discipline strongly preferred or applicable technical expertise. Military Veterans encouraged to apply.

* Strong written and verbal communication skills

* Ability to create technical documentation and produce technical and/or procedural reports

* Ability to work with engineers supporting a variety of tasks of differing complexities

* Strong working knowledge of MS Office applications (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook)

* Willingness to assist others and, when needed, to request assistance from others.
