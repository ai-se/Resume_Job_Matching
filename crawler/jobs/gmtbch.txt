Hiring Machine Operators

General machine and packing operator. Working under direct supervision You will assist machine operator, prepare or palletize, box and/or strap and label product for shipping. Identify flaw or imperfections in a product, perform basic keyboarding, ability to read tape measure, perform simple math and/or perform material handling in a warehouse environment (no fork truck involved).?

Job Requirements

Must have ability to: stand, sit or walk for long periods of time, comprehend and follow verbal and/or written work instructions and procedures, perform repetitive work, must be able to work either 1st or 2nd or moved to either as needed or required, must be a team player, must have knowledge of basic safety requirements.
