Job Title = Technician

We are looking for three experienced Technicians or General Laborers to join our growing team. Lifetime Quality Restoration & Construction is a growing restoration company based in Gahanna, Ohio. We service areas primarily within a 1 hour drive and pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and quality.
As a Technician, your responsibilities will include:
Arrive at customer site as scheduled
Inspect site and present solutions to the customer
Follow all established procedures and safety guidelines
Complete the project in established time frame
Ensure the project is complete to the customer's satisfaction
Qualifications:
Must have a valid Drivers License
Must be able to pass a background check
Must be able to lift up to 50 pounds
Salary will depend on certifications and/or experience level.
