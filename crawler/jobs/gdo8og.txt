Job Title = Machinist

WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT

Job Description
Using experienced knowledge of machining, programming, setting-up, and operating computer numerically controlled (CNC) machines. Is highly skilled at manual machines. Experienced with metal-finishing is a plus along with moderate welding skills. Highly skilled at quality assurance inspection. Able to perform machining operations according to specifications and instructions. Familiar with common field concepts, practices, procedures, and terminology of machining. Performs all functions according to company policies and procedures.
While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to stand; walk; use hands; reach with hands and arms; talk or hear. The employee is occasionally required to sit; climb or balance and stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl. The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 70+ pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, distance vision, peripheral vision, depth perception and ability to adjust focus.
Required Eye Protection at most times. (Prescription Safety Glasses may be necessary for the employee to provide for one's self).
Minor exposure to chemicals such as cutting oils and coolants.
Facility is exceptionally clean and organized with a great manufacturing and fast paced work environment.
