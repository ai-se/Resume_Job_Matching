Machinist
PrideStaff, a National Staffing System, is currently recruiting for Machine operators for a large manufacturing client. If you desire to work in a friendly and productive environment, this position is for you.
Machinist Responsibilities may include:
-Sweeping and cleaning of assigned plant work areas
-Prep product for manufacturing
-Inspect finished products
-Assist variety of manufacturing departments
Machinist Job Requirements:
-Minimum of 6 months recent experience in warehouse environment
-Ability to work independently as needed
-Accepts responsibility and accountability
-Ability to work in team environment
-Must be able to lift up to 30 lbs.
-Flexible availability
-Must be able to remain in a stationary position during entire shift
-Must be able to use basic hand tools
-Must be 18 years of age
-Must be willing to submit to a background check and drug screen
