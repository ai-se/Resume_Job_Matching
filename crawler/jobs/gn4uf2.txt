Job Title = Engineer

Top Three Skills:
Mechanical Design Experience
Technical Documentation Skills
Statistics & Statistical Software Application
Job Description: Position involves support of senior staff in the initiation, design, and delivery of projects, particularly test method development and experimental design for drug delivery systems. Employ basic engineering skills and practices to gather user requirements and translate them into technical documentation for execution. Assist with project definition by performing engineering studies and assessment for drug delivery systems. Perform field evaluations of existing systems and provide engineering design recommendations. Design and development of physical test methods with respect to container and combination product integration of drug delivery systems. Capable of machine design from initial conception through fabrication and into implementation. Capable of executing tests and recording results in technical documents as required.

TEAM SIZE:
- This position will report directly to Director of Engineering as well as coordinate with Senior Engineer. There are about 40-50 people in this group.
CULTURE:
- This is a very collaborative group that depends on each other for their success. Statistical analysis as well as hands on fabrication are both necessary in this group.
HOURS:
1st Shift, some OT as necessary.

Qualifications:
- 1-5 years Mechanical Engineering experience in a manufacturing company.
- Experience creating test methods
- Lab experience preferred
- Mechanical systems modeling
- Fabrication techniques
- Fixture design experience with Solidworks
- Validation experience
- Bachelor's Degree
+ Previous Med Device/Biotech/Pharma industry expereince a PLUS!

BEST vs AVERAGE:
- Those that have a go getter mentality and can work independently to drive project timelines and meet deadlines.
- Strong organizational skills are required.
