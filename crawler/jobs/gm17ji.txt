
PRIMARY FUNCTION:

To produce quality parts in a safe, productive and cost efficient manner creating minimal scrap.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

Operate a variety of injection presses and assembly equipment in a safe and effective manner.
Perform repetitive operations for mass production and maintain the integrity of the cell concept, working as a team with co-workers.
Perform miscellaneous general labor operations such as assembly, sorting parts and restocking cell functions.
Track and record lot information through all processes on all components required.
Track and record production, downtime, scrap and hours worked on labor sheet.
Report excess scrap issues as they arise.
Perform routine functions to support the operation of the machines.
Perform 5S activities to maintain the work area and assure quality production.
Maintain a safe working environment and report safety problems as they arise.
Other duties as assigned by Production Supervisor/Team Leader.
REQUIREMENTS:

High School Diploma or equivalent.
Proficient reading, writing, comprehension and math skills. Ability to follow instructions.
Physically able to stand for assigned shift.
Ability to climb ladders and stairs, and work off on a platform.
Ability to push/pull/bend/lift on a frequent basis exerting physical pressure.
Ability to perform repetitive motions.
