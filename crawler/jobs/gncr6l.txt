Machine Operator
We are looking for a CNC Machinist/Programmer for our valued client in Aurora. This individual will be responsible for increasing and improving our client's in-house manufacturing capabilities.
Responsibilities:

Generate new 3D models from blueprints or physical parts and edit existing models

CNC programming using CAM software and manual authoring of G code

CNC machine operation, including loading and unloading parts and tools, setting up and indicating fixtures

Design, produce and do custom work on holding fixtures

Evaluate the effectiveness and efficiency of manufacturing process as well as develop methods of improvement
Working hours: 7:00 AM - 3:30 PM
Skills:

3D modeling in Soldworks and/or Fusion 360

Generate toolpaths for CNC mills using CAM software such as Fusion 360, MasterCam, GibbsCam, Solidworks, etc.

Proficient with G&M codes and how the machine interprets commands

Ability to read blueprints and understanding of basic GD&T
Education:
High School
Experience:
Experienced
Qualifications:

Prior experience operating a HAAS CNC mill and lathe (4th and 5th axis experience a huge plus)

Fixture designs for parts with unusual geometry

The ability to demonstrate problem solving skills

The ability to work independently under minimal supervision
