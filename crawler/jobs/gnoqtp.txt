Job Title = Technician

Job Summary

The VWRCATALYST Technician is a key contributor in a fast-paced, team environment at a customer site. With a high degree of collaboration with their team, they provide the customer with required resources to support their operations. The Technician is responsible for providing a high level of accuracy, and contributing to a culture of safe work and superior customer service.

REQUIRED CAPABILITIES/COMPETENCIES

Communication

* Accurately records and communicates information to internal and external teams

* Shares with team any changes in demand to maintain appropriate service levels

* Builds positive relationships with both internal and external customers

Teamwork/Collaboration

* Works independently while contributing to the team's goals

* Contributes to a successful team environment by promoting unity and harmonization

* Willing to learn and apply new skill sets to help team and/or cover duties

Customer Focus

* Able to identify opportunities for service improvements and proactively communicate suggested actions to management

* Provides accurate, efficient service to ensure customer satisfaction

* Maintains professional, safe, clean work environment that embodies VWRCATALYST values

JOB REQUIREMENTS

Education

* HS Diploma or GED Equivalent required

Experience

* 1-2 years required experience in a customer facing environment – material handling/inventory/customer service

Qualifications

* Must be customer focused and have demonstrated customer service skills, and the interpersonal skills to manage/support/work various levels of personnel at the customer site

* Must possess strong organizational skills and demonstrate the ability to multi-task; meeting deadlines as needed

* Have the ability to work both independently and as part of a team
