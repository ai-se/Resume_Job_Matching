Job Title = Machinist

?Hone and lap pistons and cylinders using honing machine and lapping lathes. Machine assemblies using manual lathe. Adjust weights by drilling, and manual lathe or mill. Test and determine fitness of use of matched pistons and cylinders against established criteria.

?Work from prints, drawings, method sheets diagrams or other specifications in accordance with established operating procedures, and standard work.

?Work using both in the metric as well as the U.S. standard units of measure.

?Set up, operate and/or maintain production equipment, machines and tools using process documents, diagrams, and written instructions in support of production goals.

?Uses a variety of machinist's hand tools and precision measuring instruments to lay out stock for machining.

?Produces set-ups and adjustment holding tolerances to blueprint specification.

?Inspect incoming machined parts from outside vendors as necessary.

?Collect, analyze, and report data from production processes requiring basic mathematical and statistical knowledge.

?Participate in continuous improvement and lean methodologies.

?Perform duties using regular test equipment in accordance with prescribed procedures, practices, and specifications.

?Under limited supervision, receive limited instructions on routine work assignments and general instructions on new assignments.

?Communicate effectively, troubleshoot, and use good judgment when resolving problems.

?Determines material, parts, and equipment required

?Completes testing for functionality and criteria

?Other tasks as assigned.

?2-4 years of related experience

?High school diploma/GED

?Working knowledge of measuring tools.
