Job Title = Engineer

Teaching Personnel are looking for enthusiastic instructors and teachers of Engineering, for several roles across North Lincolnshire Academies (Grimsby and Scunthorpe). The students are eager to learn and would benefit from a motivated person that enjoys working in a progressive environment. This is ideal for an experienced Englineering Teacher or someone with strong industry experience lookin for a change in career.
To be successful in applying for the role you will;
Be confident in delivering to KS3 and KS4 students
Hold a child only DBS on the Update Service (or be willing to apply for one)
Be comfortable following the UK National Curriculum and planning lessons with support
Spotting and targeting needs and areas of improvement for students
In return as part of Teaching Personnel you will;
Be a part of a continually improving and expanding Academy
Receive a competitive minimum pay of £2400.00 per month
Have your own personal consultant that will help you through the application process and beyond
Access to our exclusive CPD Online Academy with various courses and resources.
There are options of progress for those teachers willing to embrace the role and show continued development.
If you are interested in this role please apply with your most recent CV
All applicants will require the appropriate qualifications and training for this role. Please see faqs for details.

All pay rates quoted will be inclusive of 12.07% statutory holiday pay.
