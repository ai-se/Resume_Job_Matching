
FULTON WAREHOUSE JOB
Helping us meet, or exceed our production goals Familiar with different packaging components Attach identification labels to finished packaged items Clean packaging containers, line and pad boxes, and/or assemble cartons Setup and make minor adjustments or repairs to assigned machinery and equipment Count and record finished and rejected packaged items. Inspect and remove defective products and/or packaging material. Monitor the production line, watching for problems such as pile-ups, jams, ect Observe machine operations to ensure quality and conformity Remove finished packaged items from machine and separate rejected items. Sort, grade, weigh, and inspect products, verifying and adjusting product weight Stacks finished packaged items, or wrap protective material around each item and pack the items in cartons or containers as specified by customer. Stock and sort product for packaging and replenish packaging supplies, Stop or reset machines when malfunctions occur, clear machine jams when safe to do so, and report malfunctions to a supervisor. Supply materials to spindles, conveyors, hoppers, or other feeding devices and unload Conducts on-going assessment of quality control and operational procedures. Cleans equipment after and between runs, ensures production line is sanitary Performs other duties as assigned by manager.
