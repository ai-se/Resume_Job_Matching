SUMMARY

Do you like the daily challenge of testing, inventorying, and reporting failed equipment for repair? In this key position on our team, you will be responsible for cross-training in surrounding Quality Control areas for assisting on a temporary basis as reflected through our business needs. A full time technician ideally brings 1 to 2 years of experience in a related field to the area you will be supporting. As a Quality Control Technician you will also be tasked with:

* Inspect audio visual equipment by setting it up, operating it, and performing preventive maintenance.

* Utilize electronic test equipment to make sure equipment is up to factory standards.

* Ensure tested equipment in complete with all required accessories and hardware.

* Review and upgrades software running on equipment as necessary.

* Refurbish equipment that is not in good cosmetic condition by cleaning thoroughly and replacing missing or damaged parts.

* Assist with re-shelving equipment.

* Assist with identifying high priority equipment needs

* Assist with maintaining QC Inbound Squares.

* Maintain an organized, clean, and neat work area.

* Foster a positive team oriented atmosphere.

* Perform other duties as assigned

REQUIREMENTS

* Minimum 1+ years' experience

* Experience with industry-standard computer applications.

* Proven interpersonal communication skills

* Demonstrated time management skills

* Proven customer service skills

* Must maintain a valid state driver's license.

EDUCATION

* High School Diploma or GED

PREFERRED

* Some college level credit
