Job Title = Engineer

SUMMARY:
Assists in design, development, implementation, and analysis of parts, systems and technical processes. Performs engineering design and analysis tasks with standard techniques. Assists more senior engineers in the preparation of plans, designs, computation methods and reports. Engage in continuous improvement initiatives.
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
Incorporates customer requirements into planning and work
Designs, develops, analyzes documents and supports testing of products, systems, or subsystems
Able to apply engineering principles in fulfilling responsibilities
Directs technical activities of assigned CAD designers and technicians
Proactively identifies and mitigates risk
Receives and follows direction
Guides work of intern(s)
Embraces initiatives
Works professionally with teammates and colleagues
Reports progress and status
Completes assigned tasks on time and budget while meeting technical requirements
Reviews customer specifications and requirements, and under direction develops designs to best support them
Implements design to cost
Implements design for manufacture and assembly
Implements tools expected by leadership
Develop BOEs
May participate in preparation of less complex proposals
Supports, communicates, reinforces and defends the mission, values and culture of the organization
Adheres to all quality and safety standards
Supports other projects and performs duties as assigned
Available for limited travel
QUALIFICATIONS:
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty and responsibility listed above satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
EDUCATION:
BS in Mechanical or Electrical Engineering from accredited school or equivalent experience require; Passed FE exam preferred
WORK EXPERIENCE:
Experience with electromechanical systems; experience in combustion heat and product application and measuring airflow; experience in the use of Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) and Variable Frequency Drives (VFD)
OTHER RELATED SKILLS AND ABILITIES:
Ability to read and understand mechanical drawings and electrical schematics and diagrams
Works independently on daily tasks under supervision of others
Possesses effective oral and written communication skills
Possesses quality orientation and high attention to detail
Possesses strong interpersonal skills and the ability to work with customers, vendors, employees and managers at all levels within the company
Possesses strong computer skills with demonstrated proficiency in standard business software packages (e.g., Microsoft Office)
Must be able to read, speak and understand English
