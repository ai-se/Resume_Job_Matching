Machine Operator

Randstad in Johnson City, TN is currently hiring for textile machine operators in Elizabethton, TN! If you have the drive to work, we will train you!

Responsibilities:

Performs production tasks that assist other production laborers. Positions parts or materials for processing. Assists in machine setup. Performs routine machine care, such as adding coolant to reservoirs of machines and lubricating machine parts. Performs feeding and off bearing activities. Feeds machine hoppers with stock and transports and packs finished products. Loads packaging machines. Verifies weights of filled containers emerging from processing or packaging machines. Removes foreign material from items on conveyor belts.

Working hours: 11:00 PM - 7:00 AM

Skills:

General knowledge of manufacturing environment and the ability to handle pressure of a high paced work environment

Basic Math and Reading skills, Mechanical Reasoning skills,Measurement skills
