Job Title = Machine Operator

We are seeking a Machine Operator to join our team! You will help oversee operational activities and ensure excellent quality control.

Responsibilities:
Oversee and coordinate plant operations and activities
Troubleshoot and resolve issues in a timely fashion
Produce quality product that is sealed and coded properly
Complete required paperwork and perform quality control checks
Perform regular tests of production line equipment and ensure correct materials being used on lines
Maintain machine and production area clenliness
Training of new employees, when appropriate
Vigilance in maintaining proper food safety procedures
Follow company policies of dress and conduct
Understand and follow Good Manufacturing Procedures in handling product and cleaning machines
Understand basic mechanics of production line
Perform machine changeovers
Qualifications:
Previous experience in plant operations or other related fields a plus
Familiarity with plant tools and equipment
Strong troubleshooting and critical thinking skills
Ability to handle physical workload
Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment
