Job Title = Engineer

Mid-level Engineers needed in our Carnegie, Pennsylvania office.
Responsibilities:

Project/task management related to the investigation and remediation of contaminated properties

Ensuring projects conform to local, state and federal regulations as applicable

Provide technical oversight, reporting, and occasional management of contractors for environmental related activities, such as completing test borings and test pits, monitoring well installation, various construction activities, soil testing and quality assurance

Perform data management and analysis, and prepare technical reports that summarize data in a technically sound, clear, and concise manner

Oversee and/or perform soil, surface water, groundwater, sediment, air and wetland related sampling, evaluations, and/or testing

Lead/manage design, project planning, and reporting activities

Take an active role in business development activities
Preferred Qualifications and Experience:
B.S. or M.S. degree in Civil or Environmental Engineering with 5 plus years of experience in a related field
Demonstrated experience in the investigation/remediation of contaminated properties
Experience in application of local, state and federal regulations to projects
Excellent computer skills with Excel, Word, HydroCAD, HEC-RAS and technical analysis software, and other computer applications
Excellent writing and communication skills required
40-hr HAZWOPER training preferred
Ability to work well independently as well as in a project team and multi-task environment
Hold a valid U.S. Driver's license, maintain a good driving record, pass a background check, and physical
