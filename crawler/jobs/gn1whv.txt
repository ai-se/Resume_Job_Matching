Electrical Engineer
Amtrak
Washington, DC

An an Electrical Engineer, you will:
- provide technical services to Locomotive shops and overhaul shops
- provide engineering solutions that extend beyond routine troubleshooting and routine diagnostic work
- develop solutions for train control systems problems onboard locomotives, cab cars and non-Amtrak locomotives and equipment

A successful Electrical Engineer will have good analytical skills, basic computer skills and attention to details.

Amtrak offers a benefits package that may include medical, dental, vision, life insurance, paid time off, retirement plans and rail privileges.
