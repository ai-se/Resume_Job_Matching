Job Title = Technician

Electrical technician capable of operating within a integration and test lab environment. Must be familiar with RF, analog, digital, integration, and test skills to support and facilitate testing of complex hardware. Lay out, build, test, troubleshoot, repair, and modify developmental and prototype electronic modules, equipment, cables and systems. Apply principles and theories of electronics, electrical circuitry, engineering mathematics, electronic and electrical testing in the assembly of electronic equipment, missile control instrumentation, test equipment and cabling. Work under direction of engineering staff. Mandatory Associates degree in electronics technology. Electrical assembly experience. Ability to comprehend electrical schematics Desired Electronics Solder certification. RF understanding.
