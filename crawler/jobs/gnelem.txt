**Job Description**

**Texwipe is a worldwide leader in contamination control products and process materials for aerospace, automotive, biotechnology, food processing, data storage, medical device, pharmaceutical, semiconductor, telecommunications and other industries requiring critical manufacturing environments.**

**Our products are purchased by the world's foremost pharmaceutical, medical device, compounding pharmacies, biologics, food processing, semiconductor and electronics, aerospace, industrial and print companies.**

**Headquartered in Kernersville, North Carolina, the company supports global customers with sales offices throughout North America, Europe and Asia. Manufacturing centers located in North Carolina, the Philippines, and China provide the highest levels of quality and manufacturing technology.**

**We currently have Machine Operator opportunities on the 12-hour rotating shifts (7:00 am- 7:00 pm and 7:00 pm - 7:00 am).**

**Job Responsibilities**

? **Monitor all process parameters on equipment.**

? **Make necessary machine adjustments to produce a quality conforming product.**

? **Enter operational data into operating system to control variables and to produce consistent material.**

? **Inspect products being manufactured and identify defects.**

? **Follow all standardized quality methods and maintain production level to meet standards.**

? **Maintain equipment in a clean and contamination control state.**

? **Maintain organized paperwork and records.**

? **Maintain safety and housekeeping compliance.**

**Job Requirements**

+ **High school diploma or equivalent and a minimum of two years related experience operating equipment in a manufacturing environment.**

+ **Strong mechanical aptitude to perform minor repairs and adjustments to industrial equipment.**

+ **Must be familiar with SPC.**

+ **Good math and computer skills required.**

+ **Good oral and written skills required.**

+ **Must be able to read and comprehend Work Instructions and Standard Operating Procedures.**

+ **Must be able to work in a critical contamination control environment, requiring the use of special gowning to include the use of a facemask and latex gloves.**

+ **Must be able to regularly stand, stoop, kneel and lift up to 40 lbs. and wear hearing protection.**
