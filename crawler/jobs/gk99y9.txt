
We are seeking a Machine Operator to join our team! You will help oversee operational activities and ensure excellent quality control.
Position Summary and Responsibilities 2ND AND 3RD SHIFT!
Ensure compliance with company safety and quality standards
Use of mold presses (small & large) to mold small plastic and rubber parts
Run material thru the machine while checking the quality of the product throughout the job
Monitor pressures, temperatures, and cure times on the injection mold presses
Assist with preventative maintenance as well as minor repairs of production equipment
Use small inspection hand tools
Complete accurate production paperwork
Load and unload equipment and materials
Provide innovative solutions to improve plant efficiency
Qualifications
High school diploma or general education degree (GED)
Minimum of 1 year of experience in Injection Molding
Excellent communication skills both verbal and written
Able to read and understand engineering drawings, specifications, and process control documents
Ability to add, subtract, multiply, and divide into all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions, and decimals
Demonstrates attention to detail, accuracy, and thoroughness.
Communicates effectively and works cooperatively with colleagues, management and other departmental representatives
