Why Join Altec?
Altec is looking for Engineers graduating in Fall 2019 or Spring 2020 to join the team! Our engineers work collaboratively to implement lean principles throughout the facility, support manufacturing and the field with existing products, and may work with New Product Development teams.

Potential Responsibilities
Design, modify, and/or apply components and system using engineering methods and techniques to meet specified technical objectives on time.
Analyze and solve complex product design, application, manufacturing, installation and service problems.
Create unique original product designs which may be patentable.
Direct other engineers and technical support personnel to carry out specific design tasks.
Analyze competitive product designs and report findings.
Implement defect prevention procedures or processes in product design or application.
Analyze new technologies for potential application in utility equipment.
Develops standards and procedures used in the design and manufacture of CAD products.
Provides technical leadership for supervisors.
