Job Title = Engineer

Design equipment according to job specifications. Travel to plant sites and measure existing equipment and make drawings of modifications and improvements for new equipment. Make design changes to equipment to improve quality and reduce manufacturing costs. Work with fabrication shop to ensure proper construction and make changes as needed. Inspect equipment before shipping to make sure construction is complete and satisfactory.
Education:. Minimum of 6 plus years in asphalt plant equipment manufacturing or other heavy machinery required. CAD experience with SolidWorks required.
