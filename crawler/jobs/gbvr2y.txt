Job Title = Machinist

Rocky Mountain Twist is looking for a Machinist to join our growing team. We are searching for an individual with the desire to do precision work, learn a skill which is in high demand, and to grow professionally within our Company. This positionwill work directly with our Senior Machinists and will be responsible for production of machined parts by programming, setting up, and operating a computer numerical control (CNC) machine, conventional lathes and mills and precision grinders. On the job training will be provided while performing responsibilities on the shop floor.
Duties:
Read prints and job orders to determine dimensions and machine set-up
Set-up and operate various machining equipment such as lathes or mills, in order to machine or grind components to precise customer specifications
Use measurement tools to ensure accuracy of parts
Qualifications:
Candidates must have a high school diploma or equivalent; have excellent math and mechanical skills/aptitude; and havethe ability and desire to do precision work. Previous experience with lathes or mills is preferred. Candidate must be able to work off shifts once trained.
