Job Title = Machinist

Description:

Will plan, setup, and operate various processing equipment, such as fabric cutting machines, autoclaves, laser projection systems, and molding equipment. Make difficult composite layups without the aid of templates to produce production and other parts using a variety of materials such as dry and pre-impregnated composites, carbon, fiberglass, plastics, foams, resins, rubber, and felts. Dry fits, installs and cures details, performs ply layups, and runs vacuum checks. Be detail oriented. Reviews own work according to company standards and customer specifications. Ability to read and understand written work instructions.
