Job Title = Engineer

Overview

Do you enjoy working with hardware and technology to build a system that meets a customer needs?

Are you passionate about technology, enjoy being hands-on, and leading others?

The Engineer is responsible for providing post-sales engineering and consulting design and implementation services related to hardware and software technologies; and providing presales support by assisting the development, review and validation of customer solutions.

Responsibilities

* Partners with business leaders to deliver services that support company objectives and that are consistent with Winning Together values.

* Assist in identifying design options for systems infrastructure.

* Supports Presales activities including:

* Analyzing technical needs, requirements, and state of a customer current infrastructure and operations;

* Identifying opportunities and developing technical solutions to meet customers' needs and requirements;

* Reviewing and validating solutions to ensure compatibility and consistency with Computacenter Fusionstorm Inc standard methodologies.

* Interacts with Solutions Architects, other Engineers and vendors to adjust designs to meet the customer's needs.

* Develop levels of effort for systems development and testing.

* Perform the build, configuration, testing and maintenance of servers, storage, virtualization and networking related hardware and software.

* Prioritize and perform research on technical problems with minimal guidance

* Identify research, analyze and resolve system malfunctions and operational problems.

* Develop design/testing/implementation and change control documentation

* Meet schedules and goals for work..

* Track project tasks against project plan and meet project achievements.

* Provide status to management on work as required.

* Communicate technical information to non-technical personnel, vertically and horizontally within an organization.

* Assist with the definition, development, installation and administration of solution demonstrations in the Computacenter Fusionstorm Inc labs.

* Possess excellent verbal and written communication skills, customer service and analytical skills.

* Work well with people from many different disciplines.

* Work with other IT support personnel and service providers to resolve technical problems.

* Rapidly learn new technologies and paradigms

* Shift flexibility

* Participate in vendor training to maintain and grow skills and knowledge of existing and new products.

* Supports Sales efforts to meet Regional financial goals.

* Understand and abide by all security and operational polices.

* Embrace and support Computacenter's mission and core values.

Qualifications

* Knowledge of a wide range of hardware and software technologies.

* Knowledge of Linux and Python basics a must.

* General server knowledge

* Understanding of server, storage, virtualization and networking technologies, procedures, operations and standard methodologies.

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or related field.

* Minimum of 2 years as a systems engineer or similar.

* CCNA, A+ certifications a plus

* Legally eligible to work in the United States

* Strong technical writing skills to develop system documentation

* Ability to build technical solution drawings

* Strong verbal and written communication skills.

* Motivated to develop and maintain skill set and product knowledge to improve value and to deliver quality services to our customers.

* Act as team lead

* Professional self-starter, able to work collaboratively

* Projects a professional image and strong business insight during customer interactions.

* Possess a sense of urgency, strong organizational and follow up skills.

* Ability to multi-task and work efficiently

* Ability to work independently
