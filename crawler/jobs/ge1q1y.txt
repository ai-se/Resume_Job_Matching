**job description**

Machine Operator

Do you like to have three to four days off per week? Would you like to work in a diverse environment that offers promotion opportunities and training while earning up to 14 dollars an hour? We are looking for entry level Machine Operators to join a local manufacturing company.

You can go to work in a few days in a clean, and safe facility with a focus on quality, not quantity. This team works hard but in a relaxed, casual atmosphere. You will need to be a quick learner and enjoy working in a fast-paced work environment. Safety is always a priority so your first day on the job will include a thorough safety orientation. You will be working for a national company that is growing and needs operators of machines that make flexible HVAC ducts.

Responsibilities:

-Follow the production schedule given by the crew chief

-Inspect the line to verify the raw materials are the correct ones (insulation, core and jacket)

-Place appropriate labels and spec sheets

-Operate the Duct Machine

-Roll the product to the bagger

-Maintain area and keep clean at all times

Working hours: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Skills:

-Be detail oriented.

-Enjoy working in a fast paced environment.

-Understand the importance of meeting deadlines based on measured output.
