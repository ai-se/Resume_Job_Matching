Job Title = Technician

Job Summary
The Dominion Energy, Southeast Energy Group, Radio Networking Team is seeking a qualified RF Technician in our Charleston area location. The qualified candidate will be responsible for safely installing, maintaining, troubleshooting, and repairing Two-Way Voice and Data Communication Systems, including both Digital and Analog Voice Radio, SCADA, VoIP Networks and Microwave. A successful candidate will often work independently, supporting day to day operations, which may include being called out after hours. Technicians may also be required to assist out of town storm restoration efforts in order to provide crews with radio communications. Required Knowledge, Skills, Abilities & Experience
• 5+ years of experience in two-way radio or related telecommunications field
• Knowledge of IP Networks as related to Radio over IP and/or Voice over IP
• Proficient in Radio and Repeater Programming
• Strong analytical troubleshooting and problem solving skills
• Knowledge of Microsoft Office products
• Proficient use of commonly used test equipment such as, but not limited to, Signal Analyzers, Watt Meters, Tracking Generators, Spectrum Analyzers, Volt-Ohm Meters, and Antenna Sweep Gear
• A working knowledge of Antenna Systems and the ability to tune and align Combiners and Duplexers
• Ability to interpret Schematics and Block Diagrams
• A clean and valid driver's license
• Must be able to lift and carry 50lbs
• Able to perform basic troubleshooting on third party network circuits and equipment Education Requirements
An associate's degree in Electronics or related technical field Working Conditions Test Description
