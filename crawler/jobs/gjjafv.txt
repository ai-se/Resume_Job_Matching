Job Title = Technician

Works under the direct supervision of the Employer Services Director, including performing drug/breath screens, employment screens and assist departmental bookkeeping and housekeeping duties, assists in all procedures, and maintenance of records and gathering of information for adequate records. In addition to these duties, this person will be trained/certified to conduct drug collections, alcohol testing, hair tests, audiometric screening, pulmonary function, and pre-employment testing. Twenty-four hour on-call is required for this position, along with transportation duties upon need. Therapy tech duties include general cleaning of equipment and laundry; assisting with treatments and patient safety as directed by therapist. Good interpersonal skills and the ability to multitask is required. Spanish speaking a plus. Some knowledge of scheduling, phone use and fax.
