Job Posting Title:

1463-Maintenance Technician

Job Description:

SPECIFIC DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Able to complete assigned jobs in a timely manner.

* Able to troubleshoot mechanical and electronic problems on assigned equipment.

* Must have electrical installation experience.

* Analyze mechanical and operations problems and take corrective action.

* Test assigned equipment, after performing repair, to ensure that equipment is safe and operational.

* Perform weekly preventive maintenance on equipment to ensure lubrications and adjustments are according to specifications for upcoming production.

* Record all preventive maintenance performed on equipment.

* Able to work any shift as needed.

* Be a self-starter and able to work with minimum supervision.

* Ensure that high standards of quality are maintained.

* Document all changes made to equipment.

* Train others on repair and maintenance of equipment as required.

* Identify and order materials for repair and maintenance as required.

* Must have basic welding experience.

* Must have own tools

* Other duties as assigned.

* Will follow established programs, policies and practices to produce safe quality foods that meet regulatory and company requirements.

* Will be accountable to TreeHouse Food Group's Food Safety and Quality Principles.

* Will support the development, implementation, maintenance and ongoing improvement of the SQF Level 3 Systems.
