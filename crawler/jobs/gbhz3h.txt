Position Description:
The machinist will machine metal parts and components to repair mechanical seals used in industrial settings, such as refineries and factories. Analyze specifications, set-up, operate, and maintain manual machines. Verify quality of finished work with precision measurement tools. Collaborate with engineers and machinists to evaluate needs, troubleshoot problems, and recommend solutions. This M-F, 7am to 3:30pm shift with frequent overtime.
· Machine 300-series stainless steel parts utilizing standard machines, including manual engine lathe, milling machine, and drill press
· Meet production goals relating to efficiency, schedules, and scrap and rework
· Verify the quality of finished work using precision measurement tools, demonstrating an in-depth understanding of specifications and workmanship
· Maintain a safe workplace by wearing PPE, following procedures and rules, and completing training
· Clean and maintain machines and tools, and keep an organized workspace
· Work closely with other machinists to discuss and solve problems relating to repairs and equipment
· Collaborate with inventory workers and engineers to status and discuss work-in-progress
· Focus on improvement by participating in continuous improvement team activities
· Record accurate, timely data on computer-based systems or in manual logs
· Apply in-depth understanding of the process flow of documents, materials, and parts through the repair or manufacturing process
· Apply knowledge of equipment applications in the manufacturing process
Position Requirements:
· Machining experience in a manufacturing or repair environment operating manual engine lathes and milling machines. More experience is highly valued.
· Able to machine parts from start to finish
· Experience in using and interpreting precision measuring instruments, such as micrometers, dial indicators, and depth gauges
· Must be able to read and interpret blue prints and drawings
· Must communicate clearly, understand instructions, and read instructions in English
· Forklift certification desirable
· Standing for long times required, repetitive arm motions, frequent lifting of 30 lbs with occasional lifting up to 50 pounds
· Able to work overtime and weekends as required on short notice
· Demonstrated track record of reliable, dependable work ethic with a personal commitment to quality workmanship
· Must show a customer-service mindset at all times
· High school diploma or GED equivalent
