Function: Product Engineering
Title: Engineer - 60821

Your Responsibilities
As an Engineer for the Product Engineering Center, located in Waterloo, IA, you will execute transmission design for 7/8/9 tractors for CI and PDP projects. In addition, you will:
Investigate warranty failures and identify root cause & solutions
Support DTAC to diagnose and repair customer tractor failures
Support Waterloo factories on large tractor production
Visa sponsorship is NOT available for this position.
What Skills You Need
2 or more years of design/testing/manufacturing experience on mechanical machines
Experience using CAD, GD&T, tolerance stack up
What Makes You Stand Out
Other areas of experience or interest that will help us place you on the right team include:
Machine design experience and specific experience with Creo, ANSYS, ROMAX, Matlab or other design engineering tools
Experience with CANgraph
Experience with NVH, specifically on gear and transmission noise
Education (or equivalent work experience)
Ideally, you will have a degree or equivalent work experience in the following:
Bachelor's degree in Mechanical or Agricultural Engineering
