Job Title = Machinist

Responsibilities:

Read and understand blueprints
Perform complex layouts
Work to close tolerances
Set up and operate manual machines such as lathes, vertical & horizontal mills, drill presses, surface grinders, rotary grinders. Operations to include turning, boring, threading, facing, grinding
Design and fabricate machining fixtures to support machining operations
Conduct close tolerance, precision, quality assurance inspections
Process documentation associated with turbomachinery machinist functions. Including, but not limited to, work scopes, inspection forms, hold tags, photo logs, turnover logs
Operate a variety of power hand tools, such as grinders, drills, porta band saw
Design, grind and sharpen cutting tools required for machining operations
Observe all safety regulations. Participation in the safety committee may be required
Maintain a clean and organized work area
Qualifications

Must have a mechanical background and a minimum of 5 years experience in the machinist trade.
Specialties should include machining projects associated with turbine and compressor repairs/modifications. The machining experience must include operation of manual machines such as lathes, vertical and horizontal mills, drill presses, surface grinders.
Operation of a tool post grinder is of benefit. Ability to perform dynamic balancing of rotating equipment is beneficial.
