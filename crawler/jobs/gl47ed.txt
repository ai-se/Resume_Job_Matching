Job Title = Machine Operator

A pharmaceutical company is looking for several new machine operators to join their growing team. This warehouse worker will operates machine to slit continuous lengths of material, such as cardboard, felt, metallic foil, paper, plastic, rubberized sheeting, or textile fabrics, into strips of specified width. Must have machine operating experience.
FIRST SHIFT HOURS
Primary Responsibilities:
Operating slitting machine including preparations for slitting and getting the material ready to be packed
Load/ Unload materials
Set up machinery
Operate Machinery
Daily Maintenance
Inspections of materials utilizing measuring tools such as calipers, micrometers, and gauges
Job Requirements:
Basic mathematical skills required.
Must communicate effectively using Basic English communication and written skills.
Ability to actively communicate with supervision regarding machine difficulties when required.
Ability to organize and manage multiple priorities, oversee the operation of multiple machines on assigned line.
Able to perform complicated machine operation tasks with minimal supervision.
Has basic working knowledge and understanding of commonly used concepts, practices and procedures within the field.
Relies on limited experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals.
Job Type: Full-time
Salary: $15.00 /hour
