Job Description:
Perform necessary pre-operation activities to ensure proper equipment startup including:
Cleaning feed tanks, weighing and adding raw materials, mixing or blending materials, and transporting feed tanks to point of use
Operator is responsible for keeping equipment running smoothly and efficiently including in-process quality checks and order changing or size changing machines. This involves disassembling, cleaning, and reassembling machines.
Visually and manually inspect finished goods for quality defects, operating the vacuum chamber and packaging/labeling of finished goods.
Operate/monitor multiple pieces of equipment during operation to ensure quality production and minimal unplanned stops.
Communicate with team members and support teams to ensure continuous production of the correct product at high quality levels with minimal wasted time and materials.
Maintain clean and orderly work area.
Perform routine maintenance
