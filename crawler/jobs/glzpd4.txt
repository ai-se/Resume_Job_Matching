
DESCRIPTION

APR has been engaged to identify hard working Machine Operators for one of our biggest clients in the manufacturing industry.
JOB DETAILS:
JOB TITLE: Entry Level Machine Operator
LOCATION: Beltsville, MD 20705
CONTRACT: 12 months or longer
PAY: $12/hr
SHIFT: ALL SHIFTS AVAILABLE
RESPONSIBILITIES:
Read process sheet and blueprint for the work order.
Process parts through the machine
Measure seals using tools such as micrometers, pi tape, gauge, venires, etc. by checking dimensions as indicated on the blueprints.
Place completed seals on the rack and take finished product to designated area
PHYSICAL/ENVIRONMENTAL DEMANDS:
All the time
Working with forming parts as stated above.
Full use of both hands
Walking
Standing 8-10 hours a day
Most of the time
Lifting 0-35 pounds approximately 3 times per day
Some of the time
Pushing and moving with assistance 50+ pounds approximately 2 times per week
Contact with dust or fumes
Bending or stooping
REQUIREMENTS/QUALIFICATIONS:
HS Diploma
COMPENSATION:
We pay weekly every Friday, have direct deposit, and offer a competitive benefits package: Medical, Dental Vision, 401k plan, etc.
BACKGROUND CHECK INFORMATION:
At a minimum, a 7-year background check, education verification, employment verification, and drug screen will be conducted upon hire. Your suitability for employment is contingent upon successfully passing these required pre-employment screenings.
