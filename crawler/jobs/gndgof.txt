Position: Engineer
Location: Lutheran General hospital, Park Ridge
PRN/Registry - scheduled on call, as needed; all shifts--looking for someone willing to work any shift as needed
Purpose:
The Maintenance Engineer performs a variety of specialized electrical, mechanical~HVAC and clinical repairs. Each individual possesses a specific expertise in at least one of the tradecrafts with a working knowledge in other pertinent areas.
Position Requirements:
High school graduate or equivalent, plus training required for stationary engineers license.
Three years of experience working with boilers, pumps, H.V.A.C. systems and associated equipment in a large institution or major facility or equivalent education.
Skilled in the proper use of power tools and test equipment
Specifics in one trade
General knowledge of maintenance, repairs, installations and procedures
Current City of Chicago, City of Elgin or National Institute for the Uniform Licensing of Power Engineers (NIULPE) stationary engineers license.
Current State of Illinois driver's license
Ability to travel.
Ability to walk, climb, push, pull, bend, and reach.
May be exposed to hazardous materials and toxic substances.
Ability to work in extreme cold or hot working conditions and confined spaces.
Ability to work on electrical circuits up to 220 VAC.
Ability to work rotating shifts and day of the week including holidays.
Ability to lift up to 35 pounds without assistance. For patient lifts of over 35 pounds, or when patient is unable to assist with the lift, patient handling equipment is expected to be used, with at least one other associate, when available. Unique patient lifting/movement situations will be assessed on a case~by~case basis.
Accountabilities:
General maintenance activities for all AMG buildings and practice sites.
Moving/relocation duties
Timely response to emergency requests for service
Coordination of work activities with requesting entities
Documentation of work activities
