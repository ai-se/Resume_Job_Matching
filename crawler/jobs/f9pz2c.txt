Job Title = Machinist

Machinist
Location:
Romeoville, IL, US, 60446
Company:
9297-Corrosion Fluid Products
Essential Functions & Responsibilities:

Machine parts to specifications using machine tools such as manual/vertical lathes, milling machines, shapers, or grinders. Additional training will be provided.

Measure, examine, and test completed units in order to detect defects and ensure conformance to specifications, using precision instruments such as micrometers.

Study sample parts, blueprints, drawings, and engineering information in order to determine methods and sequences of operations needed to fabricate products, and determine product dimensions and tolerances.

Maintain industrial machines, applying knowledge of mechanics, shop mathematics, metal properties, layout, and machining procedures.

Design fixtures, tooling, and experimental parts to meet special engineering needs.

Responsible for the final inspection on product before shipment.

Keep warehouse and shop areas looking professional and clean.

Assist others as required.

Job duties are subject to change as directed by management.
Essential Physical Functions/Equipment Used:

Must be able to lift objects weighing up to 75lbs.

Must be able to stand, kneel, and walk for long periods of time.

Must be able to, or possess ability to learn how to, operate large warehouse equipment; namely forklifts.

Must be able to work off hours, weekends, and OT on a short notice basis based on business needs.

Knowledge of rigging, lifting and moving heavy and awkward products.

Able to work in abnormal (heat/cold/clean room) operating conditions.

Knowledge of lathes, milling machines, grinders, micrometers, calipers, etc.
Preferred Education/Experience:

High School Diploma or Equivalent required.

1-3 years previous hand-machining experience required

Knowledge of required equipment, including lathes, mills, grinders, micrometers, calipers, etc.

Clean driving record.
