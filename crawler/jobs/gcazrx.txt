Job Title = Machinist

Job Summary: Under minimal supervision builds and machines parts from a blue print using a variety of manual and CNC machines. A journeyman machinist.
Minimum Requirements:
EITHER: 1) One year of technical schooling in advanced machine shop and three years hands-on experience,
OR 2) Six years working as machinist.
Safety Requirements:
Must not pose a direct threat or significant risk of substantial harm to the safety or health of himself/herself or others
Must be committed to a high standard of safety and be willing and able to comply with all safety laws and all of the employer's safety policies and rules and must be willing to report safety violations and potential safety violations to appropriate supervisory or management personnel.
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
Blue Print Skills:
Must be able to find most problems on drawings.
Must be able to read and understand all blue prints.
Math Skills:
Must have advanced trigonometry skills to solve most shop problems.
Essential Functions:
Set up and take down of projects
Machine parts under minimal supervision
Good knowledge of shop tools and machines
Safely operate overhead crane
Able to read blue prints
Maintain a clean work area
Maintain safe environment
Essential Mental Functions:
Must be able to understand implicit and explicit instructions and react favorable in all work situations.
Must be able resolve problems, handle conflict, and make effective decisions under pressure.
Essential Physical Functions:
Requires lifting of at least 50lbs, stooping, bending and stretching to accomplish assigned duties.
Requires standing, bending and stooping for extended periods of time.
