You will serve as a ENGINEER/SCIENTIST in the TDL Interoperability Engineering Branch (CODE 53525) of SPAWARSYSCEN PACIFIC SAN DIEGO CA.

This is a RE-ADVERTISEMENT of Announcement Number:DE--DMM. Applicants that have previously applied NEED to re-apply to this announcement.

This announcement uses the Cyber Workforce direct hire authority to recruit and appoint qualified candidates to certain positions in the cyber workforce.
Responsibilities

You will be responsible for providing engineering and management oversight for Foreign Military Sales (FMS) of United States Navy Tactical Data Link Systems TDLS) and associated support systems.
You will be responsible for creating, executing, and monitoring schedules and plans for the definition, development, acquisition, test and integration of the assigned system program segment.
You will be responsible for preparing price and availability documents and Letters of Acceptance.
You will be responsible for software and hardware integration of tactical communications systems including platform and interoperability considerations.
