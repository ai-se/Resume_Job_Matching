Job Title = Machine Operator

Hiring Machine Operators General machine and packing operator.
Working under direct supervision You will assist machine operator, prepare or palletize, box and/or strap and label product for shipping.
Identify flaw or imperfections in a product, perform basic keyboarding, ability to read tape measure, perform simple math and/or perform material handling in a warehouse environment (no fork truck involved).
