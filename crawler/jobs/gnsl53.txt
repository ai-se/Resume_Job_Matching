Position Description
This position is primarily responsible for maintenance and support of existing motorized actuators and manual gear actuators. This position has daily interface with the Manufacturing, Sales, Service, Quality Assurance departments and works on a variety of assignments from order engineering to design enhancements.

Duties & Responsibilities:

- Process ETO production orders

- Perform design modifications to existing components for cost reduction and compliance to specific customer requirements

- Create, revise and maintain assembly bills of material

- Provide technical support to other departments

- Evaluation and testing of new designs

- Analysis and troubleshooting of failed components

- Evaluation of dimensional variances on machined components

- Creation and maintenance of procedures, forms and technical documentation

- Assist with 3rd party hazardous location certification agency audits and documentation

- Interface with global engineering partners

Position Requirements
- Minimum Associates of Science in Mechanical ing, Mechatronics Engineering or Mechanical Engineering Technology.

- Staff: 0-5 years’ experience, Senior: 3-10 years’ experience

- Proficiency with 3-D CAD modeling (SolidWorks preferred)

- Proficiency with geometric dimensioning and tolerancing

- Travel Requirements: 5-10%

Preferences
- Great people skills

- Experience with the design and testing of 3-phase induction motors

- Experience with electro-mechanical industrial equipment in a manufacturing environment

- Experience in gear design and gear inspection

- Experience specifying materials for various applications (steels, polymers, lubricants, seals, paint, heat treating, etc.)

- Experience with casting design

- Knowledge of electrical packages and wiring diagrams

- Knowledge of hazardous location agency certification (FM, CSA, UL, ATEX)

- Experience performing failure analysis of components with and without FEA software

- Ability to multi-task, self-starter, establish priorities under guidance of supervisor

- Strong analytical, organizational and problem solving skills

- Strong communication and technical writing skills

- Ability to coordinate work efforts with Sales, Service Quality and Manufacturing

- Microsoft Office proficiency (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Project)
