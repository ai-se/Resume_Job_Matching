
Are you a skilled machine operator ready for a new challenge?
You can go to work within the next two weeks in a new, clean, and temperature controlled facility! Safety is always a priority so your first day on the job will include a thorough safety orientation.
As a Machine Operator you will set up, adjust, and monitor machines according to provided specifications. Your keen attention to detail will help you identify problems, troubleshoot, and conduct preventive maintenance to keep machines running efficiently and producing products that meet the highest quality standards.

Responsibilities:
-Gown up and comply with all cGMP and PPE guidelines.
-Read and understand all written procedures as outline in SOPs, Work Instructions and Master Batch Records (MBR)
-Set up equipment ensuring operations are consistent with current SOPs.

Working hours: 7:00 PM - 7:30 AM

Skills:
-Use designated weights from 1 gm to 250 kg to verify scales
-Scoop and dump chemicals into containers up to 1,000 kg
-Setup and operate blenders in accordance with required SOPs
-Setup and operate pin mill system in accordance with required SOPs
-Setup and operate nitrogen convey system with required SOPs (ACC only)
-Place finished containers on a scale to verify gross weights
-Use a computer and specialized program to capture production information

Education:
High School

Experience:
Experienced

Qualifications:
-Experience in a cGMP or FDA regulated manufacturing environment
-Dry product processing and packaging experience
-Demonstrated mechanical aptitude including previous maintenance experience
-Good eye/hand dexterity and coordination. Ability to perform repetitive tasks and distinguish colors
-Good math and reading skills
-Ability to work individually and as part of a team
-Ability to perform basic metric and other mathematical calculations
