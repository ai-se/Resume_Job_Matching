Job Title = Machine Operator

The Machine Operator maintains machines to keep production running by performing low level maintenance and troubleshooting.
Responsibilities Include :
Provides continuous machine support by continuously monitoring secondary equipment for up-time efficiency and equipment safety.
Planned Preventative Maintenance on Molds.
Maintains proper documentation for all materials and machines.
Supports and maintains all policies of the company including but not limited to ISO/BRC Standards, Health and Safety Requirements, Continuous Improvement, and standard work procedures.
