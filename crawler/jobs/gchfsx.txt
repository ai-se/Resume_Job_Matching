Job Title = Machinist

ITI Western USA is seeking a Machinist to join our team! You will operate and repair a variety of machines and mechanical instruments as well as produce quality hydraulic components.
Responsibilities:
Operate machine tools to produce precision parts and instruments
Modify parts and materials to improve overall production
Test completed equipment to detect and remove defects
Perform routine maintenance on machinery
Interpret drawings and follow specs
Calculate dimensions and tolerance using prints and measuring instruments
Work with a team to assemble entire products or components
Rotate through tasks for specific production processes
Conduct quality inspections on products and parts
Prepare finished products for shipment
Maintain a clean and orderly work area
Qualifications:
5 years experience in machinery and or manufacturing or other related fields
2 year experience set-up and operation on a lathe
Familiarity machine tools and equipment
Strong analytical and mathematical skills
Strong attention to detail
Familiarity with assembly tools and equipment
Ability to handle physical workload
Ability to work well in teams
Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment
Ability to travel if needed
Preferred 2 years:
Mig welding experience
Electrical experience
