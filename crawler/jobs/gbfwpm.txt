Job Title = Machinist

Job Description
A machinist will report directly to the area Group Leader. This position is responsible for the operation of machine tools of various types.

Responsibilities:
Essential Duties and Responsibilities
?Machine set up
oAssemble and install the tooling required per the set up instructions
oInstall work holding devices
oAssemble in process inspection equipment required per the instructions
oReview work instructions for errors, omissions, and clarification that may be required
oTransfer any electronic data required to the machine
oPerform the first run of the operation
oMeasure product and adjust machine as required
oSubmit product to quality for first piece inspection and approval
oInform the Manufacturing Engineer of any problems or concerns
oTrain others on the operation as requested
?Machine operation
oOperate machinery per the instructions provided
oMeasure product as it is produced to ensure conformity
oAccurately document activities as required
oMake adjustments to the machine as needed to maintain quality
oChange and adjust tooling as needed to ensure proper operation
oMaintain a clean and organized work area
oAccurately communicate conditions surrounding a failure of an operation
oAlways maintain a high level of safety practices
?Machine set down
oSubmit any work instruction corrections to the applicable Manufacturing Engineer
oSubmit any electronic data from the machine tool to the applicable Manufacturing Engineer
oRemove all data and instructions from the work area
oReturn inspection equipment to the appropriate place
oRemove and clean tooling and work holding devices. Return to the appropriate place
oReturn unused, identified raw material to the material crib

Working hours: Various Shifts Available

Skills:
Knowledge, Skills, & Abilities
- Good observation and time management skills
- Should have strong math, written,and verbal communication skills
- Good mechanical aptitude
- Ability to adapt to workload
- Ability to multitask

Education:
High School

Experience:
1-4 years

Qualifications:
Education and/or Experience
- Experience in a manufacturing environment preferred
- Use of precision measuring equipment

Environmental & Physical Demands
- Manufacturing environment. May require sitting or standing for long periods of time
- Occasional exposure to loud noise
- May require lifting up to 40 lbs
