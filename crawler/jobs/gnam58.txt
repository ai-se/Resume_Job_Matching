
We are looking to fill a CNC Operator or CNC Operator Trainee position. As a CNC Operator you will be responsible for the following:
Running CNC Lathes and Mills
Deburring
Inspecting parts with various precision tools (Micrometers, gages, tri-rolls, Johnson gages, comparator, etc.).
Changing offsets on CNCs to account for dimensional changes.
Changing tools (endmills, inserts, etc.) on CNC machines
Read Prints or Operator Sketches
Prospective employees are required to have the following:
Reliable Transportation
Ability to work 1st shift
Motor Skills
Common Sense
Ability to work in a fast paced environment.
Ability to work well with others.
Operator experience is not required, but is preferred. Compensation is based off of experience and will be determined during the interview process.
