The Systems Engineering position is part of a team that supports the requirements, design, development, test, and integration of complex, specialized hardware/software systems installed on ground and airborne communications platforms.
The System Engineer is expected to:
-Understand physical architectures incorporating voice, video, and data capability across multiple security domains
-Review and evaluate electrical wiring diagrams to ensure design details trace to system requirements
-Understand test, integration, verification, and validation planning
-Write effective verification test procedures ensuring system performance and capabilities meet system requirements
-Translate the voice of the customer into system level requirements
-Perform requirement traceability
-Develop and evaluate trade studies for accuracy and completeness
-Write subsystem specifications based on system level requirements
-Evaluate alternative solutions and establish preliminary system designs
-Review and evaluate diagrams to ensure design details trace to system requirements
-Prepare effective presentations to communicate technical information to non-technical professionals
