Job Title = Machinist

Overview:

The Machinist

supports the Salt Lake Pro Shop, handling rework and returns of pumps and

troubleshooting of machining and mechanical issues. The Machinist is

responsible for the translation of engineering and customer specs to create a

compliant part, including verifying specifications, maintaining product process

by using sound machining principles, policies and controls. Also, will

conduct specialized manufacturing engineering assignments in order to obtain

the most consistent components/products and at the most economical

manufacturing costs for manufacturing.

Job Duties and

Responsibilities:

+ Studies blue prints, sketches, drawings, or specifications to determine dimensionsand tolerances of finished work piece, sequence of operations, and setuprequirements.

+ Operates varietyof machine tools and equipment such as manual lathes, mills, drill presses and saws.

+ Performs a wide range of turning, facing chamfering, cutting off and similar machiningoperations, on a variety of castings, parts, special orders, rework, re-machining,etc. of various materials.

+ Measures, marks, and scribes dimensions and reference points on material or workpiece as guides for subsequent machining.

+ Selects, aligns, and secures holding fixtures, cutting tools, etc.

+ Makes adjustments to machines and ensures proper set up to produce qualityproduct.

+ Starts and observes machine operation to detect malfunctions or out-of-tolerancemachining, and adjusts machine controls or control media as required.

+ Inspects work using precision measuring tools to ensure all parts meet drawing specifications.

+ Keep work area clean and orderly and performs all work in accordance with establishedquality and safety procedures.

+ Performs work under general to minimal supervision depending on skilllevel/experience.

+ Other duties as assigned.
