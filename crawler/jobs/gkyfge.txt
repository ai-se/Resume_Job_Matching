Technician

Do you enjoy working with engineers in a Lab environment? Randstad is looking for a Lab Technician Supervisor to perform a broad range of technical tasks; diagnosis, maintain production equipment and systems. Relies on instruction, experience, and pre-established guidelines to perform the function of the job. Identifies, proposes, and implements approved process changes. Direct report to the Engineering Manager.

Responsibilities:

Familiar with and able to diagnose and perform maintenance and repair of Production and Warehouse tools, equipment and systems.

Will receive specialized training, in order to perform more complex maintenance/repair tasks in order to meet the needs of the department.

Basic operation and maintenance instruction/assistance to customers & other staff members in a professional manner.

Will also build Production orders, following the specifications, and assure that all such orders are built per accepted procedure and meet all quality requirements.

Duties also include creation and review of process documentation.

Involve in performance of technical processes including technical as well as data entry duties.

Maintain and update accurate documents of different lab procedures
