Job Title = Technician

Acara has an opening for a Metrology Technician for Tualatin high tech company.
Handling and cleaving (rough cleave and using a microcleaver tool) semiconductor patterned wafers.

Maintain record keeping of submissions.
Need to have basic understanding of STEM/XSEM metrology in order to correctly submit detailed requests to metrology technicians for imaging.
Required Skills / Qualifications: Minimum 2 years working as technician.
Preferred Skills / Qualifications: 1 year metrology lam experince.
