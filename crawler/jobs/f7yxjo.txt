Job Title = Machinist

Study blueprints, sketches, drawings, manuals, specifications, or sample parts to determine the dimensions and tolerances of finished the work piece, sequence of operations and setup requirements.
Must have the ability to read and interpret documents (i.e. safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, procedure manuals), to write routine reports and correspondence, and to have good verbal and written communication skills. Must be able to calculate figures and amounts and have knowledge of the metric system. - Measure, mark and scribe dimensions and reference points on material or work piece as guides for subsequent machining. - Select, align and secure holding fixtures, cutting tools, attachments, accessories, and materials on machines such as mills, lathes, jig borers, grinders and shapers. - Access CNC program and perform set up routine of CNC machine tool; run CNC machine jobs.
