Job Title = Engineer

Forensic engineering firm is looking for a Florida professional engineer experienced in building design and construction to conduct field investigations and assist with report writing.
The investigations are typically of residential condominiums, townhomes, and high rises throughout Florida in support of construction defects litigation.
The work requires travel throughout the state, climbing on roofs, ladders, riding hydraulic lifts, i.e., it is physically demanding.
It also requires familiarity with building envelope code requirements and construction practices.
The ideal candidate would be knowledgeable, ambitious and self-motivated, someone capable of working without direct supervision.
THIS POSITION IS NOT FOR MECHANICAL, ELECTRICAL, PRODUCTION, OR AEROSPACE ENGINEERS.
