Job Title = Technician

TECHNICIAN- NASHVILLE, TN
Parman Energy is seeking a motivated and knowledgeable Master Technician to join our Operations team. The Master Technician is responsible for performing and maintaining services, repairs, and installation of fuel and lubricant dispensing equipment at client or other Parman locations. Other responsibilities include performing regular facility and fleet vehicle maintenance.
Job Requirements
A strong mechanical aptitude and experience
High school diploma or equivalent
Obtain Class B CDL with Hazmat endorsement and complete industrial lift truck certification within 90 days of employment
Ability to effectively communicate and interpret information both written and verbally
Ability to compute basic mathematical operations and apply concepts of algebra and geometry
Periodic overnight travel
Experience in fuel or lubrication storage and dispensing equipment is preferred
