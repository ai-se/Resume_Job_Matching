Our Technicians aren't just "workers they are valued employees. We spend considerable time working with and training them to be highly effective and successful. The Technician's role is much more than cleaning carpet... it's Exceptional Customer Service that includes being an advisor to our customers, offering additional services to ensure 100% satisfaction. Qualified candidates must possess a valid driver's license with a good driving record, and must pass a background check. You would need to be able to lift and carry up to 50# up a staircase. Prior experience is not required.
