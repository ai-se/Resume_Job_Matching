Job Title = Machinist

Acara solutions is looking for Machinist for our Client located in Irvine, CA -Perform operations such as drilling, tapping, reaming, and boring on a variety of work requiring a degree of skill.
-Align and secure holding fixtures, cutting tools, attachments, accessories, and materials onto machines.
-Monitor the feed and speed of machines during the machining process.
-Needed to make setups on a wide variety of parts -Work from drawings and specifications using a variety of precision measuring instruments -Work under general supervision; work is subject to progress and final inspection.
-Inspect work after completed as well as complete all appropriate inspection paperwork -Cross-train / rotate to various types of machines in order to learn function in their operation.
-Perform Lean Initiatives including Continuous Improvement -The employee may be needed to work beyond normal working hours from time to time as the workload dictates -Perform other related duties as assigned.
