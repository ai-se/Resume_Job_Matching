Job Title = Machinist

Job Description:

Nesco Resource is in search of a Machinist for a full-time, temporary-to-hire position with a growing company in the Troy, NY area. The Machinist will be responsible for the set up and operation of HAAS lathes and mills to produce precision parts and instruments. This position will also fabricate and modify parts to make and/or repair machine tools. The Machinist will apply knowledge of mechanics, shop mathematics, metal properties, layout, and machining procedures to complete daily tasks.

Requirements:

1 year of previous experience working with HAAS lathes or mills required

Able to setup and operate the HAAS lathes or mills required

Able to read and understand blue prints

Strong attention to detail, able to work in a fast-paced environment

Can follow both written and verbal instructions

Steady arm and hand

Good manual and finger dexterity

Able to lift 50 pounds unassisted

Hours: 7:30am-4:00pm (Monday-Friday)
