Job Title = Machinist

**PDS Tech, Inc. is seeking a Machinist in Arlington, TX.**

**Summary:**

+ Produce high quality products and services for our customers.

+ You will assemble and inspect product to ensure the highest quality product is provided to our customers.

+ You will be responsible for your work area to ensure the safe and efficient operation of the area.

+ You will set up and monitor the production of product in your area and participate in the continuous improvement process at the site to address safety, production, and quality improvements.

**Position Responsibilities:**

+ Machinist is required to be capable of operating any equipment within our machine shop. (CNC Lathes, Drill Presses and Milling Machines)

+ Machinist is responsible to machine parts per work instructions and production packages to include loading / unloading parts from machine, fork lift driving, design and suggest new machining processes to reduce labor hours.

+ The machinist will also be capable of making new programs on the CNC machines for various new parts and configurations.

**Prior experience using these machines:**

+ Manual Lathe.

+ Tormach CNC Mill.

+ BridgePort Manual Mill.

+ Trumpf C02 Laser.

+ CNC Lathe Mori Seike With Fanuc Control.

+ CNC Lathe Hass with Fanuc Control.

+ CNC Lathe Mori Seike With Fanuc Control.

+ CNC Lathe Fryer With Siemens Control.

+ Ability to communicate information clearly.

+ High School Diploma.

+ Ability to analyze information and concepts.

+ Ability to collaborate with others and work independently.

+ Ability to identify key details.

+ Ability to troubleshoot.

+ Good coordination, motor skills, and dexterity.

+ Creative thinking and idea generation.

**Prerequisites:**

+ High School Diploma or equivalent (GED) required.
