Job Title = Engineer

Under the direct supervision of the Engineering Manager, the engineer will be responsible for performing general maintenance and repairs on facility equipment and property structures, in addition to performing grounds maintenance. Below are the outline of the responsibilities but not limited to these:
Perform minor building, HVAC, plumbing, electrical and painting repairs as necessary
Implement preventative maintenance measures
Maintain inventory of repair equipment and supplies
Complete inspections of offices with Engineering Manager
Respond quickly in the event of an emergency, notify appropriate personnel and follow safety protocol
Perform other maintenance duties as requested
