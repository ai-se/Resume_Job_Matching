Job Title = Engineer

The successful applicant will be responsible for streamlining packaging processes, creating technical documents and procedures, explaining complex information in a clear and concise manner and working with subject matter experts and technical staff to ensure accuracy of process descriptions.
Primary job responsibilities include: • Partner with senior operations staff to translate legacy process knowledge into optimized operational procedures and documents
• Quickly learn the details of existing packaging processes
• Analyze existing packaging processes and recommend improvements
• Plan and develop operational procedures and documents
• Determine the needs of users of process documentation and ensure those needs are addressed
• Work with staff to make process documentation easier to use
