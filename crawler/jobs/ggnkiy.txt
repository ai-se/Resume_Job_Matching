Job Title = Engineer

The Position
The Mechanical Engineer will work on the design and development of Supercritical CO2 Fluid Extraction Systems. The candidate should have experience with machine design. Experience in pressure vessel or high-pressure equipment design preferred. Experience in SolidWorks required, and experience in SolidWorks add-ons such as routing and simulation is preferred.
3-5 years of experience in machine design required.
Responsibilities and Duties
Develop equipment and component designs to meet customer and market requirements, while meeting applicable safety and design standards for high-pressure equipment.
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, and respond to developing requirements of one of the fastest-growing industries in the world.
Troubleshoot products, subsystems, or components for continual product improvement
Revise or redesign existing products to reduce cost, increase manufacturability and reliability
Provide technical support to manufacturing, marketing, purchasing, and customer assurance to ensure conformance to company and customer requirements
Learn and understand Green Mill technologies
Attend occasional conferences (some travel required)
Requirements
Strong expertise in SolidWorks including the ability to route tubing and cabling, work with weldments and sheet metal components.
Experience in creating models/drawings for machined components, weldments, and assemblies (including tubing/wiring).
Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering required (equivalent industry experience considered on a case-by-case basis)
