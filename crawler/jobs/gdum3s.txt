Job Title = Machinist

AMEWAS, Inc., a growing Department of Defense (DoD)/ Federal Contractor, is seeking highly motivated and talented Machinist. This position is on a SCA contract which is subject to the terms and conditions of the McNamara-O Hara Service Contract Act (SCA).

A typical day as a Machinist may include:
This position manufactures parts and items of equipment from raw stock of different kinds of metals, metal alloys and other materials, through the use of machine tools and precision handwork.
The work requires the set up and operation of most types of conventional machine tools such as lathes, vertical and horizontal boring mills; bench, plain, or universal milling machines; shapers; planers; internal and external grinders; thread grinders; and radial drills.
The work frequently requires the use of a variety of auxiliary machine tool attachments such as rotary vises, dividing heads, taper attachments, magnetic chucks, vertical milling heads, tapping attachments, rotary tables and others.
May participate in planning for manufacturing, kitting, or repair items by determining facilities, equipment, material and number and type of personnel required.

Physical Environment and Working Conditions:
The physical environment requires the employee to stand and walk throughout his/her workday, often bending over and picking up items less than 50 lbs. and/or operating machinery to assist in the movement of heavy objects such as fork lifts and dollies.
While performing the duties of this Job, the employee is regularly exposed to moving mechanical parts. The employee is frequently exposed to outside weather conditions and risk of electrical shock. The noise level in the work environment is usually loud.
