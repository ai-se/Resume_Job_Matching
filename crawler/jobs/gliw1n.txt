Job Title = Technician

US Tech Solutions is seeking aTechnician\"for 12 months with a client inMinneapolis, MN

Description:
This position is in the RTG-Implantable, Systems Integration and Test function whose charter is to provide engineering development and support of characterization, integration, verification and validation strategies for new product development. This position is principal level, working with an interdisciplinary team of systems engineers and technicians to execute test plans/protocols and other laboratory work in support of system level testing of RTG Implantable products.

Position Responsibilities:
Develop and execute characterization, integration, verification and validation tests and support analysis of data to understand capability.
Author test documentation, data collection and result reports in written form using GDP.
Perform troubleshooting of lab equipment and/or test set-ups and lead corrective action efforts and implementation of a solution.
Ensure personal understanding of all quality policy/system items that are personally applicable.
Follow all work/quality procedures to ensure quality system compliance and high-quality work.
Maintain lab, including equipment calibration and qualification.
Author equipment input forms and proposals for new test equipment.
Under little or no supervision installs new equipment.
Work collaboratively with engineers to evaluate and improve design.
Report status and results of work on a regular basis.
Communicate clearly and effectively in a timely manner.
Subject matter expert of and perform testing involving electrical, hardware, firmware, software, and/or telemetry interfaces.
Under little or no supervision, design and document devices, machines, fixtures, assembly methods, test systems, and components with little or no engineering input.
Identify opportunities for system level test automation.
Perform job functions under little or no supervision.

Basic Qualifications:

Education Required

2 years trade school with additional advanced certification and applicable prior work experience.
Minimum 8 years experience in medical device laboratory and test setting.
Familiar with lab equipment like Oscilloscope, Fluke meter etc.
Workable knowledge of scientific and/or laboratory techniques.
Good verbal and written English skills.

Desired/Preferred Qualifications:

2 year Associate Science Degree, (Electrical or Bio-Medical discipline or similar preferred).
Ability to perform technical activities more complex in nature with larger impact.
Ability to work under little or no supervision and follow general direction for work.
Ability to build and test mechanical models, electrical circuits and assemblies.
Experience with Equipment Qualifications and/or Test Method Validation.
Experience with systems engineering practices and testing.
Experience with Microsoft Office.
