Job Title = Machinist

Description
Job Summary:
The machinist will work as part of the direct manufacturing shop floor team. In this position, the machinist set up, maintain, and operate multiple machines ensuring efficient productivity in a manufacturing environment. The machinist be responsible for calibrating and clean machines, make adjustments, and feed raw material or parts into a machine. The machinst will control and adjust machine setting and test operation of the machine periodically. This position requires the operation of machinery in a temperature controlled manufacturing environment.
This position requires the ability to read instructions and follow these instructions with precision to produce outputs.
Essential/Key Functions:

Set up, calibrate, adjust, and run multiple machines at one time

Change blades as necessary

Control and adjust machine settings (e.g. speed)

Inspect parts with precision and measuring tools

Test operation of machines periodically

Check output to spot any machine-related mistakes or flaws

Keep records of approved and defective units or final products

Maintain activity logs

Understand specifications of the task at hand and the desired result by reading blueprints, mechanical drawings etc.

Inspect and measure finished products and compare them with requirements to determine if the process has been completed properly

Check and maintain machinery daily to ensure functionality

Read and comprehend instructions and follow established procedures

Make size adjustments and understand measuring instruments

Take precision measurements using micrometers

Check output to ensure highest quality

Maintain equipment in good condition and perform operator preventative maintenance

Submit requests for maintenance repair as required

Keep records of production quantities and time

Report on issues, malfunction or defective parts

Able to stop production if the work center/machine is not producing products that meet all required specifications

Properly identify all product processed with work order number, inspection status, and operation status

Follow all safety guidelines and operate machinery in a safe manner

Physical presence on the job is essential and necessary including weekends and holidays if deemed necessary by the company

Other duties as assigned by management Position Requirements
Minimum (Required) Qualifications:
Education and Experience

High school diploma or equivalent;

Technical Degree a plus
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities

Proven experience as a mill, rough grind or saw operator preferred

Skill in operating diverse high speed machinery and precision measurement tools (calipers, micrometer etc.)

Ability to read and blueprints, schematics, & manuals

Mechanical aptitude

Must have ability to operate a forklift

A keen eye for detail and results-driven approach

Technical knowledge

Ability to use and read a micrometer and precision measuring tools

Must possess basic math skills

Ability to understand quality control principles

Good communication skills (verbal and written)

Ability to follow all safety guidelines
