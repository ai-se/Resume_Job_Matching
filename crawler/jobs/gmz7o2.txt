Job Title = Technician

About the role Balfour Beatty has an exciting opportunity for a Technician to join our Highways team to work in Rockbeare, Devon.
What you'll be doing As a Technician, you will have the following responsibilities: * Identification of maintenance works * Identify works and create works orders * Provide safety information for works identified * Maintain programme of and undertake RMMS inspections, if applicable * Maintain RMMS data base, if applicable * Undertake Safety Inspections and Patrols, if applicable * Undertakes Routine and Specialist surveys including lamp scout as required * Carry out Inventory surveys when directed * Monitor works undertaken by workforce and subcontractors * Carry out site audits and field inspections * Act as Duty Officer for Winter Maintenance and Emergency Response * Maintain Roadspace requirements and Network Occupancy requirements, if applicable * Populate as NOMs as required * ESDAL and AIL Management, if applicable * Deputise for Route Manager when required * Assist with the production of the programme of works * Assist with Public and third party liaison * Assist with the production of TTROs * Assist with the coordination with HE and other HE areas and Local Authorities * Assist with the production of works estimates * Assist with the production of Carbon Calculator and Environmental KPIs Who we're looking for You will have the following qualities and experience: * Have a track record in network management of highway contracts * Have experience in specifying and execution of highway maintenance works * Have extensive experience in the inspection and maintenance of highway assets * Be committed to the business core values listed below * Competency (training may be applicable) * Full driving licence * CSCS1 * 12A/12B Safety Day (minimum) * Winter Maintenance Decision Maker * NOMs * RMMS Health and Safety Training CITB SSSTS * Environmental Training CIWM Waste Awareness Certificate Why work for us Day in, day out, our teams deliver amazing projects in challenging conditions; developing, managing and maintaining the vital infrastructure and public spaces that modern economies, societies and communities rely on.
