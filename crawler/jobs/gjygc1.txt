Job Title = Technician

WANTED BEVERAGE EQUIPMENT TECH (IN Shop Technician) with some REFRIGERATION EXPERIENCE a plus. Call to schedule a Interview with Andra! .
We are ideally looking for someone with diagnostic and repair skills for Industrial Coffee Machines. Someone who is passionate about fixing mechanical and technical, plumbing, electrical, Refrigeration and maintenance problems would be ideal. A successful Service Technician will have strong attention to detail, be self-motivated and well-versed in all maintenance and repairs.
Pay is based on experience
Essential Job Functions:
Provide and document complete diagnostic test and repair or replacement services to customers
Perform electrical, plumbing, and appliance maintenance
Track all parts and materials used in repairs or replacements
Keep supervisor appraised of work progress
Ensure that the final work product meets quality standards and is inspected by supervisor or designee
Maintain a safe and clean work area for customers and coworkers
Perform other miscellaneous duties as assigned
Essential Job Skills:
Knowledge of ( Electrical, Plumbing, Maintenance, etc.) is certainly helpful but, we also consider applicable work experience
Refrigeration experience or related fields are helpful
May lift up to 50 lbs and/or move up to 100 lbs. with assistive devices
Potential exposure to heights and hazards related to working with electrical
Possible exposure to environmental conditions include heat, cold, humidity, noise, dust and wetness
Prolonged periods of standing, stooping, and bending Must furnish own hand/shop tools
Valid driver's license
Job Type: Full-time Monday - Friday 8:30-5:00
