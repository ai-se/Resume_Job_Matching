TECHNICIAN/PRODUCTION
DUTIES & REPONSIBILITIES:
• Tool setup/Tear down
• Assembly of products in accordance with instructions
• Document completion of operations on production traveler
• Tool Repair/Maintenance
• Tool Certification
• Lift 5 to 15 pounds
• Able to use hand tools
• Tooling Specialists has many responsibilities, not limited to the above
