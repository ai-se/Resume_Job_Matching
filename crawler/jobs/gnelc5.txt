Machine Operator

Up to $100 sign on bonus!*

Do you enjoy hands on work and have an eye for detail? We are looking for Machine Operators to work for a local Oil Manufacturer. This is a temp-to-hire position with no experience needed. This is a great opportunity to develop new skills and gain experience in a manufacturing environment. Multiple shift times are available.

You can go to work within the next two weeks in a fast paced, team work based facility. This team works hard but in a relaxed, casual atmosphere. Safety is always a priority so your first day on the job will include a thorough safety orientation.

*Sign on bonus contingent upon the successful completion of 3 weeks on assignment

Responsibilities:

As a Machine Operator you will set up, adjust and monitor machines according to provided specifications. Your keen attention to detail will help you identify problems, troubleshoot and conduct preventive maintenance to keep machines running efficiently and producing products that meet the highest quality standards. You will be on your feet throughout your shift and will need to move swiftly to complete tasks in a timely manner, while always maintaining a focus on safety.

Working hours: 6:00 AM - 2:30 PM

Skills:

-Machine setup and machine tending

-Inspect and adjust machine to correct for defects in product

-Handle product and raw materials

-Follow directions in a dynamic work environment

-Maintain concentration over a long period of time

Education:

High School

Experience:

Entry Level

Qualifications:

-Must be proficient in math, writing skills and verbal communication

-Able to perform minor maintenance on production equipment and assist technicians during regular scheduled maintenance.

-High school diploma or GED required

-At least 2 years of recent machine operator experience

-Current certification required

-Dexterity

-Demonstrated concern for safety and good track record

-Willing and available to work overtime on occasion

-Technical or Trade School Degree, preferred
