Job Title = Engineer

The Lane Construction Corporation is a leading U.S. construction company specializing in the transportation, infrastructure and energy industries. Lane's unique combination of capabilities includes public-private partnerships/innovative financing and joint ventures; large, complex design-build and bid-build projects; and the ability to produce and install asphalt, aggregates and concrete.
Job Description
Summary:
The Engineer is responsible for design, development, implementation, and analysis of technical products and systems.
Responsibilities:
Including but not limited to:
Gather and record project quantities in quantity book
Input quantities into JDEdwards
Ensure those in the field have the most current information
Update project diary on daily basis
Maintain erosion/environmental permit compliance paperwork
Manage utility locations and maintain compliance paperwork
Perform quantity takeoffs
Coordinate procurement and delivery of materials and onsite storage
Gather QA/QC material certifications for submittal
Qualifications:
Minimum 2 years experience
BS in Civil Engineering or Construction Management
Scheduling experience highly desired
Strong working knowledge of Excel and Word
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
JDEdwards experience is strongly preferred
Strong prioritization and organizational skills; detail-oriented and able to multitask
Has knowledge of commonly-used concepts, practices, and procedures within a particular field
Relies on instructions and pre-established guidelines to perform the functions of the job.
Lane Construction offers a comprehensive benefits package with an excellent opportunity to grow and be part of one of the most respected names in the construction industry. Women and minorities are encouraged to apply. Lane is an Equal Opportunity Employer M/F/D/V.
