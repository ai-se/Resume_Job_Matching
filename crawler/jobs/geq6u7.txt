Job Title = Engineer

Responsibilities:

* Perform preventive maintenance and repair duties on all building mechanical systems, equipment, and tools.

* Work with Chief Facilities Engineer in coordinating projects and scheduling work.

* Effectively report and correct unsafe work practices or unsafe property conditions.

* Design and implement cost-effective equipment modifications to help improve safety and reliability.

* Communicate with guests/customers to resolve maintenance issues.

* Perform other related duties as assigned.

Qualifications:

* Must consent to and successfully complete a pre-employment drug screening and background check.

* Must be 21 years or older (required by Casino and/or State regulations).

* Must be able to obtain a Louisiana Racing Commission License. (Company will provide application.)

* Must have Engineer state certification number by the board of Engineers.

* Preferred 1-3 years of general repairs and/or maintenance related work experience.

* Must have a Valid Driver's License.
