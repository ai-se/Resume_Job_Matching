Job Title = Machinist

Duration: 6 months or longerPay Rate: $25/hr.Shift: M-F, 2nd shift: 2:00pm to 10:30pm, OT required
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
Perform operations such as drilling, tapping, reaming, and boring on a variety of work requiring a degree of skill.
Align and secure holding fixtures, cutting tools, attachments, accessories, and materials onto machines.
Monitor the feed and speed of machines during the machining process.
Required to make setups on a wide variety of parts
Work from drawings and specifications using a variety of precision measuring instruments
Work under general supervision; work is subject to progress and final inspection.
Inspect work after completed as well as complete all appropriate inspection paperwork
Ability to cross-train / rotate to various types of machines in order to learn function in their operation.
Perform Lean Initiatives including Continuous Improvement
The employee may be required to work beyond normal working hours from time to time as the workload dictates
Perform other related duties as assigned.
POSITION CRITERIA:
Knowledge & use of shop mathematics
Knowledge of Geometric symbols
Able to read and interpret drawings & schematics
Experience using a variety of measuring tools / instruments
BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:
High School Diploma/G.E.D
Minimum three (3) years previous work experience as a Machinist
Steel toed shoes and safety glasses required
COMPENSATION:
We pay weekly every Friday, have direct deposit, and offer a competitive benefits package: Medical, Dental Vision, 401k plan, etc.
At a minimum, a 7-year background check, education verification, employment verification, and drug screen will be conducted upon hire. Your suitability for employment is contingent upon successfully passing these required pre-employment screenings.
