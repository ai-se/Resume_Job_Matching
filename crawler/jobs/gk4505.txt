
Responsibilities for Machine Operator jobs include but are not limited to:
Operate press, laser, shot, and/or assembly equipment
Perform quality checks on products
Stack & load products for transport
Complete all job functions following proper safety requirements
Report defective material or questionable conditions to production supervisor

Candidates must meet the following requirements to be considered for Machine Operator jobs:
-A minimum education of a high school diploma or GED which needs to be verified
-7 years of verifiable work history
-Steel toed boots

* Weekly Pay - you receive a paycheck every week
* Casual, Manufacturing Work Environment - dress casual and enjoy a casual, laid back atmosphere
* Comprehensive benefits after 1 week - medical, dental, vision, options available
* Generous Bonus Incentives offered for referrals!

The schedules for these positions are 2nd or 3rd shift 6-7 days a week.
