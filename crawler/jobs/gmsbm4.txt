Job Title = Technician

Candidate must be able to read blueprints. General technician and fabrication knowledge -abrading, masking and prepping/cleaning aerospace parts.
Assist in the creation and fabrication of high reliability products and components.
Pref. proficient use of basic shop arithmetic. Experience with ISO requirements, standards, and compliance issues.
Work on efforts to get honeycomb panels and other aerospace subsystems ready for the Wet Chem area to be painted/ chromated.
Performing routine maintenance of shop equipment.
Handling and managing fragile aerospace materials and assemblies.
Basic chemical analysis for basic immersion etching and cleaning line.
Work to established LEAN practices/ guidelines.
Ability to adhere to written procedures * Experience with ISO requirements, standards, and compliance issues.
Experience in Aerospace manufacturing environment
Qualifications:
High School Diploma Required. At least 1-2 yrs of hands on technician experience working in production or fabrication.
Experience in an Aerospace manufacturing environment a plus but not required.
Experience in the preparation and application of silicate, polyurethane, two part epoxy paint systems a plus but not required.
Able to read and work to written instructions, blueprints, or shop drawings
Experience with ISO a big plus.
Environment:
Working in a spacecraft production environment. Hands on.
Top 3 skills:
1) Hands on fabrication experience. Ideally aerospace products.
2) Experience with panel layout and production.
3) Working with plans and schematics required.
