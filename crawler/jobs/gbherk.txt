This position is responsible for confirming raw materials, purchasing finished goods, factory supplies and any other items that require purchase orders in a timely, efficient manner with required follow-up inquires to insure that the production schedule is met for service requirements to customers.
Major Tasks Performed:
The employee will become part of the Purdy paintbrush manufacturing team in the maintenance department. Purdy manufactures professional painting tools of the highest quality. This position fits and assembles parts to make repairs on the facility’s various machines. Being able to respond to the needs of department managers for emergency repairs in a quick and professional manner is crucial.
Safety is a core value at Sherwin-Williams operations. It is a job requirement for all Purdy employees to participate in our safety program.
Employee involvement and communication is essential for continuous safety improvement in all areas of operational excellence: Safety, Quality, Service, Cost, and People.
This position is responsible for fitting and assembling parts to make repairs on the facility’s various machines. Provides support with mechanical repairs to ensure the ongoing integrity and reliability of equipment in order to keep the plant operating safely and efficiently.
• Fit and assemble parts to make or repair machines.
• Perform equipment repairs, modifications, installations and moves.
• Timely response to the needs of department managers for emergency repairs in a quick and professional manner
• Maintain production equipment in good operating condition while optimizing equipment uptime.
• Obtain parts from the storeroom.
• Study specifications of machines.
• Quality check parts to ensure they are of the precise size, weight, etc.
• Set up and operate various machine tools.
• Shape, form and cut metal for parts.
• Performs general welding and milling.
• Operation of the lathe, surface grinder, soldering, and brazing equipment.
• Perform routine or related duties assigned by the Plant Manager, Operations Manager, Engineer Manager or Maintenance Manager.
• Use of various tools in a safe manner.
• Support site housekeeping and safety programs, maintaining a work place that is clean and safe.
• Installs new equipment and follows all training and communication procedures when new machines are installed as well as when current machines/processes are modified.
• Understands basic electrical and pneumatic diagrams, familiar with 12 VDC to 480 VAC electrical wiring or circuits and capable of diagnosing 115 VAC control circuitry.

Basic Qualifications:
- Must be able to read, write, comprehend and communicate in English
- Must be at least 18 years of age
- Must have the legal right to work in the U.S. without needing sponsorship now or in the future for employment visa status
- Must have at least 3 years of industrial maintenance experience
- Must have knowledge and understanding of LOTO (lock out tag out) procedures
- 5 years experience as an industrial machinist
- Intermediate to advanced computer skills, including e-mail, MS Word and MS Excel
- Experience using MRP (Material Resource Planning) and MPS (Material Production Scheduling) systems.
- Machinist NCRC Certification
