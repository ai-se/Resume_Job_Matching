Job Title = Machinist

A Machinist will be responsible for:
- Fabrication of machined parts, tooling and fixturing
- Machine parts from blueprints, sketches and verbal directions
- Programming and operating of conversational milling machines and set up and operating of Haas Vertical Machining Centers
- Fill out work instructions properly
-Keep work space clean and organized
- Safely use all machine shop equipment to include saws, drill presses and manual lathes
- Meet all inspection requirements

Compensation: Hourly
Hours: Shifts are expected to be between 4-10 hours at a time between 6:00 am - 4:30 pm, NO weekends.
