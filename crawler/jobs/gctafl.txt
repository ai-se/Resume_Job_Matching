Position Title: Technician

Position Description: Our Eastside client is looking to bring on a Technician to join their growing team with solid troubleshooting skills to help with building prototypes and running test with oscilloscopes.

Job Responsibilities:

Prototype assembly utilizing both verbal and written instructions
Build and modify test set ups
Execute and document results of tests assigned by engineers
Organize and maintain engineering lab equipment, tools and supply stocks
Execute and document results of design verification tests following written test procedures
Setup, execute, and record results for ad-hoc engineering tests, building/procuring the necessary equipment, test hardware, cabling and pneumatics/tubing
Use common electrical tools and equipment to make and record measurements of critical component/assemblies parameters and characteristics
Job Qualifications:

Requires a high school diploma or equivalent
2 year electronics/technician degree, or higher
5 years min working as an electronics technician in an engineering lab environment
Fine Soldering- ideally surface mounted and IPC-A-610 certified
Component-level troubleshooting skills
Preferred, 2 years+ working within a medical device development environment, or other quality system regulated development environments
Competent with Excel and Word, experience with Mac OS preferred
Ability to design and build electro-mechanical prototypes and test fixtures
Ability to organize, procure and manage materials for projects
