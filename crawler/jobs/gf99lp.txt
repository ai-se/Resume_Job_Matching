**job description**

Machine Operator

Are you a skilled Creeler ready for a new challenge? Do you like working in a textile and yarn manufacturing environment with some overtime? A local manufacturing facility is looking for skilled ring spinners to become a part of the team. Potential career position.

You can go to work within the next few days. This team works hard but is in a relaxed and casual atmosphere. Safety is always a priority, so your first day will consist of training and orientation.

Pay starts at $10.50/hr and there is overtime as needed based on production. There is also an increase once you go permanent on specific shifts and the future opportunity for growth within the company.

Responsibilities:

As a ring spinner, you will be working with raw materials and working with empty and full packages of yarn.

You will be working on your feet throughout your shift and will need to move swiftly to complete tasks in a timely manner, while always maintaining a focus on safety.

Working hours: 7:00 PM - 7:00 AM

Skills:

* Handle product and raw materials

* Working with packages of yarn

* Tying ends together to prevent breaks

* Dexterity

* Follow directions in a dynamic work environment

Education:

No Degree Required

Experience:

Entry Level

Qualifications:

* Experience in a manufacturing environment is preferred, but not a must

* Must be flexible and able to work overtime as needed if called
