Job Title = Customer Engineer - Burlington MA or Houston TX or Remote

BMC helps customers run and reinvent their businesses in the digital age by tackling their IT management challenges, championing their innovation, and celebrating their success.

Every BMC employee has the potential to have a tremendous impact on customer success—and when customers thrive, we all do.

BMC offers bold and fearless career-seekers like you the opportunity to expand your skills, your network, and your horizons as you work to enable customer growth and innovation every day. You will be surrounded by peers who inspire you, drive you, support you, and make you laugh out loud, in an environment that fosters individuality, respect, and personal ambition.

This role involves working with a small, high performing team on Server Automation products built ground up to solve complex problems for enterprise IT teams. This job is for a talented developer who has a strong sense of ownership, can make decisions in a fast pace environment, influence change, and enjoys working with customers.

Responsibilities

This position is a customer facing role where user satisfaction is the top most priority. You will work with Support and the customer on product issues to troubleshoot, diagnose, and understand them to help bring timely resolution. This work will involve fixing defects, developing creative solutions and workarounds to problems, or enhancing/improving features based on discussions with product management.

As part of this role, you will also work with a large, globally distributed development team to perform requirements analysis, design, develop and test commercial software. Software development experience with a proven track record and practical experience with agile methodologies is required. You will be exposed to real world deployment and usage scenarios from customers and product managers and help translate them to product features that drive the value of the product. You will work with other internal groups like QA and technical writers throughout the development cycle.

Qualifications

* 8+ years of experience in software development, preferably enterprise software

* Senior level knowledge in Java and a good understanding of databases (SQL Server, Oracle, or Postgres)

* Experience in Datacenter, provisioning and virtualization handling

* Experience with Windows and UNIX platforms and application servers like Tomcat/Jboss

* Experience using source control tools like Github and build tools like Jenkins and Maven

* Strong experience in customer case and escalation handling

* Strong troubleshooting and debugging techniques and understanding of web-based technologies.

* Excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills

* Ability to work both independently and as part of a team
