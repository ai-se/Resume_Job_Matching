Job Title = Sr. Full Stack Software Engineer

Profile Overview:

Were looking for a Sr. full stack software engineer who thrives on getting their hands into everything - whether its dealing with large amounts of data on the back end (Java, Ruby, Python, .Net) or presenting it on the platform (React, Redux, Node, Backbone, or Angular).This role will be reporting to reporting to Head of Product Development and Innovation.
Requirements

Heres what you will bring:
Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related fields.
6 to 9 years of professional software development experience with Java, Python, Ruby,.Net or other object-oriented programming languages.
Strong hands-on experience and understanding of object-oriented programming, data structures, algorithms, and web application development.
Experience working on HTML, CSS and Popular JavaScript frameworks such as React, Redux, Node, Backbone, Angular etc.
Real passion for developing team-oriented solutions to complex engineering problems.
Thrive in a dynamic, rapidly changing environment and value end-to-end ownership of projects.
Excellent verbal and written communication skills to collaborate multi-functionally and improve scalability.
Interest in committing to a fun, friendly, expansive, and intellectually stimulating environment.
As a Sr. Full Stack Software Engineer, heres what you can do at Trantor:

Design, develop, and maintain performant and scalable full-stack applications.
Understand project requirements, elicit feedback, and refine solutions accordingly.
Interface with project managers, clients, and other partners and vendors in delivering solutions & troubleshooting technical issues.
Rapidly fix bugs, solve problems, and proactively strive to improve our products and technologies.
Manage individual project deliverables on industry coding standards and design techniques.
Demonstrate a willingness and ability to learn new systems and technologies as required.
