Job Title = Associate DevOps Engineer

Infrastructure is what powers every technology organization. We rely on our infrastructure to empower our customers around the world to improve the care of their patients. We’re seeking a hungry engineer who can join us on this journey, using his or her technological skills to make us more efficient. In this role, you will help to modernize our infrastructure, automate our deployment pipeline, and build tooling to ensure all systems are healthy. From day one, you will have an immediate impact on the day-to-day efficiency and security of our infrastructure, and an ongoing impact on our overall growth.
We have multiple DevOps positions posted. Please select the position which most closely matches your qualifications.
Responsibilities and Activities:
Build out new (and convert existing) cloud infrastructure as code
Build out new (and convert existing) logging and monitoring infrastructure
Manage and extend our CI/CD pipeline
Participate in multiple scrum teams as a matrixed expert
Build automation to improve workflow, increase test coverage, and improve reliability
Build automated test of the production systems and infrastructure
Diagnose development and production issues to propose and/or develop solutions
Resolve development and production database performance issues
Participate in on-call rotation
What you should be proficient at:
Working with cloud solutions with AWS, GCS, or Azure
Working with containers – Linux and/or Windows
Networking concepts in both copper and the cloud
Rudimentary Linux and/or Windows system administration
Excellent communication skills – both written and verbal
Excellent analytical skills – root cause and remediation
Required Knowledge, Skills & Abilities:
Running applications in containers (Docker)
Cloud console (AWS, GCS, or Azure)
Proficient in scripting languages (bash, Python, PowerShell, Javascript)
Proficient with configuration languages (json, yaml, hcl)
Using source code management - git preferred
Experience and enthusiasm for automating anything that must be done more than once
Strong creative and analytical thinking
Preferred Experience
Pertinent professional certifications
Provisioning systems (CloudFormation, Terraform)
Configuration Management (Puppet, Chef, Ansible, Salt)
Virtualization (Hyper-V, Vagrant, VMWare, LXC, VirtualBox)
Monitoring (Prometheus, Logstash, Kibana, Grafana, Nagios)
Load Balancing and routing (F5, Nginx)
Agile tools (GitHub, GitLab, JIRA, Trello, VersionOne)
