
Software Guidance & Assistance, Inc., (SGA), is searching for an LTE/Telecom Lab Test Engineer for a Contract assignment with one of our premier Consumer Products clients in Bellevue, WA.

Responsibilities :
Executing Mission Critical Push to Talk (MCPTT) IOT/Lab test plan for feature or issue reproduction in 3rd party Carrier/Vendor labs.
Develop strong system knowledge required for MCPTT services and call flows.
Defect troubleshooting in IOT/LC lab environment with network and operator engineers.
Perform test plan analysis, SW download, sanity testing, and configure devices specific to test plan/feature, device logging with tools
Tracking and Follow-ups on the issues for early closure with respective owners.
Learn and Create device sanity test plan for new MCPTT features Ex. ProSE/D2D
Device log analysis (SIP/IMS/Modem) and provide logical conclusion to issues after co-working with R&D Dev teams.
Support technology trials related to new feature validation by Lab/Live network testing, and integration testing
Strong verbal and written communication with R&D, Project Managers, Carrier/customer SMEs
Required Skills:
3-7 years of relevant experience in IMS/VOLTE/SIP/RTP/RTCP/LTE/Protocol domain (UE/handset side preferred).
Working knowledge of various protocols and technologies such as LTE/IMS/SIP/VoLTE/VoWifi/RCS/RTP/RTCP/MSRP/DNS/HTTP.
Strong troubleshooting and defect analysis skills and should be able to segregate issues from device and network perspective.
Ability to write and review technical reports that can be explained to the customers and technology partners.
Strong presentation skills desired.
Strong TCP/IP fundamentals and working knowledge.
Ability to work in cross functional team environment.
Hands on experience of defect tracking systems.

Must be able to work in radio lab environment.
Operate a computer keyboard and view a video display terminal more than 90% of work time.
Visual acuity, color distinction, and numeric and character detail distinction for the analysis and preparation of statistical reports and information.
Lift, move, or adjust general office equipment, boxes, or materials weighing up to 25 pounds using proper materials handling equipment and procedures.

UE/handset side experience preferred
