Job Title = Site Reliability Engineer

You will set a new standard for task management, through good communication with your team, with your manager, and your peers. You will have the opportunity to define how middleware developers can successfully adopt new structure, reduce noise, and focus on using the platform's services more efficiently. Additionally, you will be developing standardized tools for information management by the Data Engineering team.This job requires you be able to work some degree of autonomy, in collaboration with other system engineers and at the direction of the Senior Manager of Data Engineering. Because your work will have visibility by other department directors and upper management, frequent communication with your manager concerning projects and issues is expected. You are great at:
•Work with department data service administrators (e.g. MSSQL, MarkLogic, Elasticsearch) to establish/improve active monitoring of the host services and develop methods of identifying performance issues and/or usage anomalies.•Collaborate in the planning phase of any new or modified business operation that employs the use of the hosts in your domain of responsibility. •Establish/maintain server security guidelines, practices, and department procedures. •Establish/maintain effective customer points of contact (development, infrastructure, teammates) for collaboration and troubleshooting projects related to existing technical issues. •Learn, maintain, improve, and develop new methods of automation to address regular patching, upgrades, and monitoring.
•Has as deep knowledge of mixed operating system environments, structured, non-structured and distributed data platforms, and can easily apply the concepts of one data service API to another.•Has good experience scripting in one or more Unix/Linux/GNU scripting languages, including interacting with RESTful APIs.•Deep understanding of Windows Server, Redhat/CentOS, and Ubuntu Server in a production environment.•Can work work directly with project managers, infrastructure engineers and software developers.•Good experience in multiple scripting languages for systems administration•Experience in Ansible is a plus
