What you will be doing!

Independent Software is seeking a SIGINT Dataflow/Systems Engineer that will be a member of a Systems Engineering team focusing on dataflow engineering relating to deployed customer systems. The ideal candidate will have a history of direct involvement with successful efforts and system upgrades in which new data flows are evaluated and validated. This candidate will implement and supervise data management procedures for both classified and unclassified programs and project information as defined by the Program Data Manager. The candidate will assist in the implementation of operating methods to improve processing, distribution, data flow. The candidate will Identify and acquire new data sources, as well as create and manage business metadata for published data sources to ensure that it is easily discoverable, and meaningful to information workers.

Required Skills:

* Twelve (12) years of systems engineering experience in programs and contracts of similar scope, type and complexity

* Minimum of three (3) years of hands-on experience working with Apache NiFi (NiagraFiles)

* Experience authoring Systems Engineering documentation; this includes requirements analyses, and the ability to research highly technical subject matter, organize information from multiples sources, and express technical information in written form that is comprehensible to a wide audience of readers

* Experience in process mapping, group presentation, written and verbal communication, analytical, and organizational skills

* Proficiency creating documents using Microsoft Office (e.g., Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Access, Visio, and Outlook)

Education and Experience: A Bachelor's Degree in an engineering science or information systems management from an accredited college or university is required. Candidates without a degree may still be considered if they possess an additional four (4) years of systems engineering experience.

Clearance: THIS POSITION REQUIRES A CURRENT TS/SCI CLEARANCE WITH POLYGRAPH!
