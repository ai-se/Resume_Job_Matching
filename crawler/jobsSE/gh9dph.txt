What you will be doing!

Independent Software is seeking a Senior Network Engineer to provide assistance in all aspects of network management from network design through implementation, maintenance and upgrading of existing networks. Must be capable of creating network specification and related documents that would allow for the implementation of communication systems that support the distributed functionality of a software engineering environment. The selected candidate will analyze network characteristics (e.g. traffic connect time, transmission speeds, packet sizes and throughput), troubleshoot problems and recommend procurement of, and/or modifications to network components. Primary emphasis on Cisco Systems, and at least some Juniper and IBM BladeCenter (SwitchModule) experience. Need to serve as the team's authority with all network issues and tuning.

Required Skills:

* Ten (10) years of network engineering experience in programs that encompass system architecture, requirements analysis, process execution and evaluation

* Experience in developing networks from design through implementation

* Experience with Cisco lOS or JunOS or equivalent

* Experience in configuring multi-vendor network equipment

* Experience with industry leading types of network testing applications and equipment

* Experience in routing protocols

* Experience with Ethernet trunking and configuring Ethernet VLANs

* Basic Linux command line skills required

Certifications: The selected candidate must meet 8570 IAT II requirements to include one of the following certifications: CCNA Security, GIC SP, GSEC, Security+ CE, SSCP, CASP CE, CCNP Security, CISA, CISSP, GCED, GCIH.

Education and Experience:

A Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Electrical Engineering, Mathematics, or a similar engineering science is preferred for this position. Candidates with an Associate's Degree in an approved engineering science may still be considered if they possess an additional six (6) years of network engineering experience. Candidates without a degree may also be considered if they possess an additional eight (8) years of network engineering experience.

Clearance: THIS POSITION REQUIRES A CURRENT TS/SCI CLEARANCE WITH POLYGRAPH!
