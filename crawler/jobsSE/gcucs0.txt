Someone who is passionate about the core concepts of performance engineering and applies them in day to day interactions with the rest of the software development team while coming up with and executing tests and while debugging potential bottlenecks. The individual will work closely with software engineers, cloud engineers, and business analysts.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities: (other duties may be assigned)

* Ensure quality for our customers by validating Performance, Scalability, and Resiliency in our software solutions by designing, developing and executing tests, frameworks, tools, stubs, mocks.

* Participate in the constant improvement of our Performance Test Automation practices in CI/CD pipelines

* Debug, troubleshoot, and work with team members to find and fix software defects/bottlenecks using application monitoring tools

* Work with business and other stakeholders to determine and define non-functional requirements

* Create reports that describe detected defects and possible causes
