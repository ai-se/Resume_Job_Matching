
Senior Network Engineer
Rochester, MI OneStream Software LLC

Benefits Offered Vision, Medical, Life, Dental, 401K
Employment Type Full-Time

ABOUT THE JOB
The Senior Network Engineer will be responsible for designing, deploying, and securing the network of our organization. The ideal candidate will ensure solutions and designs providing optimum infrastructure uptime and security. This role requires strong technical skills and hands-on experience ensuring network infrastructure is operating within the design specifications.

Primary Duties and Responsibilities:
Perform operational analysis of new technologies and recommend necessary changes for architecture and infrastructure design
Design, plan and implement projects, as needed, to support the day-to-day production requirements
Build foundation and define capabilities and processes to address network needs in tactical and strategic terms
Evaluate existing internal environments and make recommendations for improving environment
Develop, initiate, maintain, and revise policies and procedures for networking solutions, including routers, switches, firewalls, and other networking equipment
Proactively monitor systems and perform preventative maintenance and updates to prevent performance impacts to systems and business functions
Provide operational and technical support to ensure ongoing stability and optimization of the server environment
Motivate and grow out the networking team to its next level of maturity, agility and scalability
DESIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE
Formal Education and Certification
BS/BA in Computer Science, Network Security, Engineering, or Technology related field

Knowledge and Experience
7-10 years of experience of hands-on network engineering/design and operation of large, high-availability, global enterprise environments
Experience with overall network infrastructure including LAN, WAN, VLANs, routing, switching, load-balancing, monitoring
Strong understanding of network security including firewalls, proxies, VPN
Experience configuring and deploying Cisco firewalls, routers, and switches (Cisco Meraki preferred)
Configuration of Port Security
Layer 2/3 switching
Network and application security device configuration and management (Cisco, Barracuda, Palo Alto, SonicWall)
Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) preferred
Any certifications such as, Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) and Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) Foundation are a plus
