Job Title = Cloud Service Operations Engineer - Norfolk

Cloud Service Operations Engineer
Must have AWS and Linux Experience
Location: Denver, Colorado

ABOUT THE JOB
Cloud Service Operations team is an elite team in Software AG which champions cloud technology and supports Software AG Cloud SaaS offerings. We thrive to keep our cloud products in high SLA standards, with strict data security, and top performance through continuously improving processes.
The new team member should manage & operate Software AG Cloud environments, mainly ARIS Cloud.

Tasks and Responsibilities:
Provide professional, courteous and prompt technical support to customers and internal staff with the goal to achieve highest level of customer satisfaction
Work closely with Development Teams, Cloud Security &Compliance, Sales, Global Support Organization and Product Management, to strengthen cloud offerings
Support security and compliance requirements of the Software AG Cloud Platforms
Apply findings to improve the cloud deployments and management of systems
Communication with the Software AG Cloud customers in a very customer oriented manner
Main focus will be ARIS Cloud

ABOUT YOU
Academic Degree: University degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or similar

Professional Experience in year(s): 3

Special Knowledge:
Amazon AWS Solutions Architect with experience in EC2, S3, RDS, VPC CloudWatch, CloudTrail, CloudFormation, etc.
Familiar with other cloud IaaS vendors such as Microsoft Azure, Google or Alibaba
Software automation skills
Preferable: ARIS Installation, administration and configuration experience

IT Skills:
Strong Linux administration skills - preferred CentOS
Good familiarity with Logging & monitoring procedures and the use of tools like Gray logs, Zabbix, Grafana, or Splunk
Database Management experience - preferred Oracle DBMS
Single-Sign-on, LDAP, Active Directory - configuration knowledge
Understanding of Network administration (VPN, firewalls)

Additional Requirements/ Soft Skills:
Willingness to be available for on-call duty at weekends
Motivated to collaborate in an international team with customers all over the world

WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT
The employees are smart, friendly and welcoming
Health Insurance starts your first day on the job
Three weeks paid vacation & three weeks paid sick leave
9 paid holidays (including a floating holiday and a community service day)
401(k) Plan with up to 5% employer match
Business Casual office
